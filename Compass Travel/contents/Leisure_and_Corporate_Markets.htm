Leisure and Corporate Markets

Leisure Market 

Leisure travel often occurs during family vacations where families travel to noteworthy locations such as Alcatraz Island, Iceland, London or Mesa Verde in Colorado.

Corporate Market 

Some of the larger companies will have a dedicated Corporate Travel department that is staffed with employees that make the travel arrangements for the company’s employees. Many companies need to make corporate travel arrangements but simply cannot justify maintaining a full time staff to support this role.

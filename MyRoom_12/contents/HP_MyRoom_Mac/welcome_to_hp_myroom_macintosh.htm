Welcome to HPE MyRoom Mac Help

HPE MyRoom provides a secure meeting space equipped with audio and video for chat and face-to-face contact with business contacts, friends and family. It is available 24 x 365 and is easy to install, run and use.

 

There are several versions of HPE MyRoom. Please go to the HPE MyRoom Features page to find out about each one as well as upgrades to assist in your meetings.

 

In this Help function, clicking on a link will bring up the Help page for that link.

 

To reach HPE MyRoom, go to the MyRoom home page in your browser.

 

To set up a new account:

 

  1. Select the desired language for HPE MyRoom from the drop down list next to the flag in the bottom left hand corner of the browser window.
  2. Click the Download link. Your Operating System will automatically be detected and the correct file will begin to download.

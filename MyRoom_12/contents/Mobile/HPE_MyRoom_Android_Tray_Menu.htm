MyRoom Mobile Rooms tray menu

Once you have logged in to your account, the MyRoom tray appears.

 

Your list of rooms opens.

   
Tap the menu buttons on your tablet screen to see the menu options available here.

   
The menu appears.

   
Log out: Tap Log out to close and exit the HPE MyRoom app.  
   
Settings: Tap here to see the Settings menu.  
   
Help: Tap Help to launch this help documentation.  
   
Upload Logs: Touch Upload logs as directed by an HPE MyRoom Support Agent. Fill out as directed by the Support Agent and tap Send logs.   For more information, see the next page.

   
About: Tap About to see information about the HPE MyRoom app.

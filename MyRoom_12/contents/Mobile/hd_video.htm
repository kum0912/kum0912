Allow UDP/peer to peer networking

Change your settings to suit your use. If you disable Allow UDP networking, it will force a connection to TCP networking only. If you disable Peer to Peer, it will force connection only via server for two people on the same network. Those options are helpful when you might be having some network issues, to try and switch to a different connection type. These are two of the options available to ensure the best MyRoom experience.

 

Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile.

 

Tap Settings to bring up your Settings window.

 

 

 

 

 

Allow UDP networking and Allow peer to peer networking are both selected by default. Your current networking settings are displayed at the bottom of the Settings window.

 

 

When these are turned off, the network settings at the bottom will change accordingly depending on your network connection to another person in the room.

Show network health

Change your settings to suit your use. Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile.

 

Tap Settings to bring up your Settings window.

 

 

 

 

 

Turn on Show network health to see a visual representation of how well your network is performing. If necessary when troubleshooting, a Service Desk team member may ask you to turn this on.

 

Tap the arrow next to Settings to return to your room.

 

 

When this option is on, you will see the Network health indicator next to the exit arrow in the top left hand portion of the room. Five bars are optimum network health, one is very poor.

Use USB video

Change your settings to suit your use. You can choose to use your audio through the speaker on your phone or through a headset or earpiece, if available. Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile. The Use USB video option is available only for Android devices at this time and will only display in the menu when a USB camera is connected and allowed through the system popup.

 

Tap the More button (three dots) and then Settings to bring up your Settings window. 

 

 

 

 

 

The Settings window opens. The Use USB video option will only display in the menu when a USB camera is connected and allowed through the system popup.

 

Use USB video by default is set to off. Tap or slide to turn it on.

 

Tap the back arrow to exit the Settings menu.

 

 

When you turn on the video by clicking the camera at the top of your room, the camera menu appears. You can take a picture or rotate the camera 90 degrees clockwise. 

 

 

Tapping the rotate icon rotates the camera 90 degrees clockwise.

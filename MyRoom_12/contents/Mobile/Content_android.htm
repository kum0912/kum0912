Content

Tap Content in the menu to display the content currently loaded in the room. Content can be uploaded by Presenters using the MyRoom Windows, Mac or Linux client.

 

 

Tap a folder to display its contents if the content you want is in the folder. Tap the content item you would like to display. You will see your selected content in the thumbnail on the lower right.

 

Tap the back arrow next to the word Content to see the content you have chosen in full screen.

 

 

 

Your content item is displayed.

 

 

When a Presenter turns on video or shares a desktop, and you choose a content item, you will see all three in your window, with two displayed as thumbnails. An example is at right.

 

Tap the thumbnail you want to see in the center. This applies to video, sharing and content. The item currently displayed will be reduced and your choice enlarged. Your view does not affect the way others see the items in their window.

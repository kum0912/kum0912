Invited to room or chat

When you are in your MyRoom account, another MyRoom user can drag you into a room or a private chat, with your permission.

 

Invite to room

 

When another MyRoom user invites you into a room using drag and drop or Invite to room, you will see a message as shown at right. Accepting the invitation closes any room you are currently in.

 

Tap No if you do not want to join the room, Yes if you want to accept.

 

 

 

If you accept, you will be placed into a room with the person who invited you to that room.

 

 

When a MyRoom user invites you to a chat, you will see a message as shown at right.

 

Tap No if you do not want to chat, Yes if you want to accept.

 

 

After tapping Yes, you are placed in a chat with the other person.

Settings

Change your settings to suit your use. Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile.

 

Tap Settings to bring up your Settings window.

 

 

 

 

 

The Settings window opens. Play chat sound is enabled by default. If you have a large event, this is a choice that you can turn off to avoid large numbers of notifications as people chat while in the room, especially if you are presenting.

 

Tap the arrow next to Settings to return to your room.

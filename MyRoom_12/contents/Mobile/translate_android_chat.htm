Translate chat

Change your settings to suit your use. Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile.

 

Tap Settings to bring up your Settings window.

 

 

 

 

 

If you have the Translate chat capability purchased for your account, you will see the Translate chat option. Slide the option on to have your chats translated from the originator's language to your language.

 

 

When this option is on, you will see both parts of their chat, first in the original language, then in your language. The translated chat appears in a yellowed box with your language as you have set it in brackets.

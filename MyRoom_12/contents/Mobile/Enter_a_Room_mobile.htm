In a room

Tap the name of a room to launch it.

 

In a room, you will see the microphone and speaker icons and the Menu button.

 

Your speaker is on by default when you enter a room, the microphone is off (muted) by default. Tap the microphone when you want to speak to unmute.

 

 

When you tap the Menu button, the menu at right appears.

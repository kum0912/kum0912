MyRoom Mobile chat

Once you have logged in to your account, the MyRoom tray appears.

 

Your list of rooms opens by default.

   
Tap Contacts to change to the Contacts tab.   Tap your contact's name to start a chat with your contact. You can start a chat with those in your list marked Available, Away or Offline.

   
Your chat room opens.

   
You are now in a chat.

   
Type and enter to start your chat. Your contact receives a message like this asking permission to start the chat.

   
You can call your contact in the chat for an audio conversation by tapping the Call button.

   
Your contact then receives a request to start the audio call.

   
You are now able to talk and text with your contact in the chat.

Show/hide offline contacts

Use the More menu to show or hide offline contacts. If you have many contacts, hiding offline contacts can make it easier to search for those available for you to meet with.

 

On the Contacts tab, tap the More menu (three dots) to bring up the Contacts More menu.

   
Tap Hide offline contacts so that only contacts who are signed in to their MyRoom account will display in your list.    

   

If you want to see all your contacts, both offline and online in MyRoom, tap the More menu and choose Show offline contacts.

   

All your contacts are displayed.

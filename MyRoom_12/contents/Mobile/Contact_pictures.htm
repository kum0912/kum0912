Show/hide contact pictures

Use the More menu to show or hide contact pictures displayed on your Contacts list.

 

On the Contacts tab, tap the More menu (three dots) to bring up the Contacts More menu.   Tap Show contact pictures to display your contacts' pictures.

   
You see the profile pictures of those of your contacts who have uploaded them in their accounts.    

   

If you want to hide your contacts' pictures, tap the More menu and choose Hide contact pictures..

   

Contact pictures will not be displayed.

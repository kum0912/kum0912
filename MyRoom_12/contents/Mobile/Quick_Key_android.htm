Quick key

Tap Quick key in the menu to generate a key valid for 10 minutes that you can give to others to allow them to enter your room.

 

 

You will see the 6 digit Quick key displayed. Tap OK to close the Quick key window.

 

Give the Quick key to those you want to enter your room. The key is valid for 10 minutes.

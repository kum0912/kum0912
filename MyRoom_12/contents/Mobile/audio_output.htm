Audio output

Change your settings to suit your use. You can choose to use your audio through the speaker on your phone or through a headset or earpiece, if available. Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile.

 

Tap Settings to bring up your Settings window.

 

 

 

 

 

The Settings window opens. Audio output is set at Default for the device you are using.

 

 

Tap the choice window to bring up the picker. Tap your choice to make your change.

 

 

Your audio output is now set.

 

Tap the arrow next to Settings to return to your room.

Message duration

Change your settings to suit your use. Please note that the Audio Output setting is not available for tablets in HPE MyRoom Mobile.

 

Message duration can be changed for how long messages will show up on your screen when people enter or leave the room or chat.

 

Tap Settings to bring up your Settings window.

 

 

 

 

 

Tap the choice window to bring up the picker. Tap the duration you want your messages to display.

 

 

 

Your message duration is now set.

 

Tap the arrow next to Settings to return to your content.

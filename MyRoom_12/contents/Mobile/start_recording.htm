Start recording

Tap the menu to start recording in the room.

 

 

 

 

Enter a name for your recording and tap Save and start recording.

 

   

While a recording is being made in the room either by you or someone else in the room, you will see the recording box in the room as at right.

 

You can pause the recording by tapping the pause button . Tap the stop button  to end your recording.

Chat

Tap Chat in the menu to open the chat function. Chats in the room are persistent until deleted by a Windows, Mac or Linux client.

 

 

Enter your text in the Type your message here field at the bottom of the screen. The keyboard appears when you tap into the message field. Your texts appear with a turquoise background.

 

Tap the Back arrow to close your chat.

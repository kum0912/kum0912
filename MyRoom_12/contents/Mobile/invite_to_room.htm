Invite to room

Tap Invite to room in the menu to invite someone in your Contact list to your room.

 

 

Enter email addresses separated by a space or a semi-colon (;) and hit Send when you have finished.

 

There is a limit of 100 email addresses that can be sent at one time.

   
If the person you are inviting does not have a MyRoom account, you will need to choose whether to send them a participant or presenter key. 

 

 

If your contact is online and either Available or Away, you will see the message at right.

 

Tap OK to close the message. You can also send an email message to your contact by tapping the link.

 

As soon as your contact accepts the invitation to the room, they enter the room with you.

 

   

If your contact is offline or on Do not disturb, an email is sent to them instead of a pop-up message.

 

As soon as your contact accepts the invitation to the room, they enter the room with you.

MyRoom Mobile tray

Once you have logged in to your account, the MyRoom tray appears. There are two tabs in the tray, Rooms and Contacts. By default, the Rooms tab displays when you log in.

 

Rooms created in your account are listed here, including any rooms in which you have a role.  

 

Tap a room name to open the room.

 

 

The room opens showing the number of people currently in the room.  

 

You enter the room in this manner as a Presenter and can now chat with other attendees, see and edit content, raise your hand, generate a key for others and see a shared screen as well as use audio in the room.

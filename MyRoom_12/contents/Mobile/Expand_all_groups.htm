Expand or collapse all groups

Use the More menu to show or hide all contacts by expanding all your groups.

 

On the Contacts tab, tap the More menu (three dots) to bring up the Contacts More menu.  

 

Tap Expand all groups to display all your contacts in all your groups.

   
Your groups are expanded.    

   

Tap the More menu and Collapse all groups to hide all your contacts.

   

You can expand or collapse only one group by tapping the arrow next to that group.

Add contact group

Use the More menu to add contact groups.

 

On the Contacts tab, tap the More menu (three dots) to bring up the Contacts More menu.

   
Tap Add contact group to add a new contact group to your contact list.   Tap in the Contact group field and enter a name for your new group. The arrow will turn green as you type in the field. Tap the arrow to add the new group.

   
Your new contact group is listed alphabetically in your contact group list.

   
NOTE: You must be logged in to your MyRoom account on a PC or Mac to move a contact into a group.

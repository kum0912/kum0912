MyRoom Mobile Contacts tray More menu

Once you have logged in to your account, the MyRoom tray appears.

 

Your list of rooms opens by default.

   
Tap Contacts to change to the Contacts tab.   Tap the More menu (three dots) to bring up the Contacts More menu.

   
Tap Add contact to add a new contact to your contact list.

   
Enter your contact's email address and tap the arrow.

   
If your contact does not have a MyRoom account, you will see a message similar to that at right.   Tap Yes to invite the contact through email.

   
Your contact is added to your Contact list.

   
Your contact then receives a request to start the audio call.

   
You are now able to talk and text with your contact in the chat.

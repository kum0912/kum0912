Add contact

Use the More menu to add contacts.

 

On the Contacts tab, tap the More menu (three dots) to bring up the Contacts More menu.

   
Tap Add contact to add a new contact to your contact list.

   
Enter your contact's email address and tap the arrow.

   
If your contact does not have a MyRoom account, you will see a message similar to that at right.   Tap Yes to invite the contact through email. Tap Cancel to cancel the invitation.

   
Your contact is added to your Contact list.

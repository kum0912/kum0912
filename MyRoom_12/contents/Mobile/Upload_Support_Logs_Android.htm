Upload logs

Tap Upload logs in the menu to start the upload process. You are required to fill out three of the four fields: your name, email address and either your phone number or the Case ID provided by a Service Agent. Enter any details you want to include by typing in the text area. Tap Send logs to send the logs.

   
You will see the message as shown at right when the upload is complete.

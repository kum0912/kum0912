Welcome to HPE MyRoom Mobile Help

HPE MyRoom provides a secure meeting space equipped with audio and video for chat and face4-to-face contact with business contacts, friends and family. It is available 24 x 365 and is easy to install, run and use.

There are several versions of HPE MyRoom. Please go to the MyRoom Features page to find out about each one as well as upgrades to assist in your meetings.

In this Help function, tapping a link will bring up the Help page for that link.

To reach HPE MyRoom, go to the HPE MyRoom home page.

 

On your Android device, go to Play Store and download the HPE MyRoom Mobile app.

 

Tap Install and then Accept to complete the installation.

On your iOS device, go to the App Store, search for and install HPE MyRoom.

   
Once you have accepted permissions and the app is installed, tap Open to launch HPE MyRoom.

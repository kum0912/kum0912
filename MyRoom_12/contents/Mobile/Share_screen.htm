Share screen

Tap the menu and tap Share screen to share your screen to others in the room.

 

 

 

 

You will see a warning message that everything on your screen will be shared. You can click Don't show again to avoid this message in future. 

   

When you are sharing your screen, you will see a small green area saying Sharing screen.

 
   
Tap the menu and tap Stop sharing to end your screensharing session.

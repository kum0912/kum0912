Delete

You can use the Edit button to work with the displayed content item.

 

Tap the Edit button to display the menu. Tap the Delete button to delete all annotations in the content area.

 

 

 

 

 

Confirm your deletion of all annotations by choosing OK. Tap Cancel to cancel the operation.

 

NOTE: Be careful when using the Delete option. You cannot undo the deletion.

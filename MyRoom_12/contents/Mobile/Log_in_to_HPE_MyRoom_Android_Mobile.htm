Log in to MyRoom Mobile

From the Log in page, you can either log into an already created HPE MyRoom account or use a key to enter an event or room directly.

To log into your HPE MyRoom account, your account must already have been set up on a PC or Mac to log in to HPE MyRoom on your Android iOS tablet or phone. Enter your account name (your email address) and touch the Next arrow.

 

 

 

Enter your password as set up when creating your account.

Touch the Next arrow to enter your account.

If you forget your password, click the Forgot my password link to start the password reset process. Watch for an email from myroom@hpe.com with the password reset link.

 

After you have reset your password, enter your new password and complete the login process again.

 

   

If you have previously installed MyRoom Android Mobile, when a new version is available you will receive the notification shown at right after you log in to your account. Tap OK to start the download and install the new version.

   
If you only have one room associated with your account, you will automatically enter that room. Otherwise, you will see a list of your available rooms for you to choose.

   

Log in using a key  

 

You can use a key provided by a presenter or meeting organizer to enter a room directly without having a MyRoom account. Touch Enter with key to start.  

 

Enter your name as you want it displayed in the room and then the key you have been provided. Touch Enter room.

 

 
You enter the room associated with that key. You enter the room with the role defined by the key you used so with a Presenter key, you enter as a Presenter, with a Participant key, you enter as a Participant. You can see content, video, shared screens and send/receive chats.

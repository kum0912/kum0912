Delete a contact group

Before deleting a group, all contacts within the group must be removed. Move them to another group or delete the contacts.

 

  1. Right click on the name of the group to be deleted.

  2. Click on the Delete contact group choice.

  1. Confirm the deletion of the group by clicking Yes or cancel the operation by clicking No. This operation is permanent and there is no Undo.

 

NOTE: The original Contacts group cannot be deleted until another group has been created.

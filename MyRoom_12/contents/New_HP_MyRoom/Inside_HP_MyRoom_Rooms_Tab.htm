HPE MyRoom - Rooms tab

Click on the Rooms tab to see your rooms. Your first room is automatically created for you in the Rooms tab. The icons and tabs are as follows:

 

Icon

Name

Comments
Change status icon Click on small arrow or Status to view choices.
Contacts tab Click to see a list of contacts.
Rooms tab Click to see a list of rooms, owned or assigned.
Create new room icon Always enabled on the rooms tab.
Menu Click to choose to show only favorite rooms.
Room key Click to generate a room key for others.
Schedule or view upcoming events for the selected room Brings up the Events window for the selected room. Not visible until a room for which you have scheduling permissions is selected.
MyRoom Settings icon Always enabled.
Help Menu icon Always enabled.

 

Click on an icon to access help for that icon.

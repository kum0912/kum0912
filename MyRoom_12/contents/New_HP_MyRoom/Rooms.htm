Rooms right click menu

Your first room is created for you in your new account. You can create rooms up to the maximum available in the product you are using. For all users, when the maximum number of rooms is reached, the Add option disappears from the right click menu as described below. The menu below appears when you right click on a room. Right clicking in the Room panel gives a reduced menu.

 

Menu Choice

Name

Comments
Enter Enter the room you have right clicked.
Add room Add additional rooms to the maximum allowed for your product.
Add room to favorites Marks the room you have right clicked as a Favorite.
Delete a room Appears when a room is selected. Click to delete a room.
Rename room Appears when a room is selected. Click to rename a room.
Add room group Add Room Groups to organize and classify your rooms.
Move to room group Move the room you have right clicked to one of the room groups listed.
Manage access or Release room access Appears when a room is selected. Click to grant access privileges to another user or to release access to another person's room.
Generate Quick, Presentation or Persistent keys Use these keys to give different levels of access to a room, depending on your particular account permissions.
Events Create, modify and notify regarding Events.
Log out and Exit Click to log out or exit from HPE MyRoom.
     

Clicking on Log out will log out of HPE MyRoom but leave the application available to be logged in with another user name or at another time. HPE MyRoom can be launched again by clicking on the icon in the taskbar.

 
Clicking on Exit will close HPE MyRoom and remove the icon from the taskbar.

 

Click on a menu choice above to access help for that choice.

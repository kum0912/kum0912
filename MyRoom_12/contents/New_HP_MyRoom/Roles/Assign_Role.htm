Assign a role

  1. Choose the role to assign to the contact and click the Send offer button or click Close to cancel the operation. Hovering over the role in the list brings up a tooltip with the description of the abilities for each role.

     

Role

Ability
Owner Owns rooms, can do anything with that room including renaming and deleting the room.
Reader Can view content but cannot add or delete content.
Writer Full control of content - can view, add, and delete content in room.
Room Administrator Full control of content and can invite users into room.

 

 

 
  1. Once you send the offer, the contact's status remains as Pending.

Manage access with roles

You can assign roles to contacts so that others can use the room when you, as the owner, are not available. For instance when you cannot attend a meeting but all the meeting materials are already in that room, a Reader, Writer or Room Administrator can assist. The different roles are defined as follows:

 

Role

Ability
Room owner User who created the room. Can add, modify, and delete content. Can assign permanent access to room through Manage access. Can delete the room.
Room Reader User added to a room for permanent access. Can view and display content in left or center viewing area for others to view, email others in the room, and control some functions in a room. Cannot add new content, delete content, manage users or access the events page for that room.
Room Writer Can view, upload and display content in left or center viewing area for others to view, can create audio and video messages, notes and whiteboards. Cannot invite other users or access the events page for that room.
Room Administrator Has many of the same capabilities of the room owner. Can manage content and invite other users to this room. Cannot modify room, manage user access to the room, delete all room content, create persistent keys or delete the room.
 
A complete list of what each role can do is found here.

 

To assign a contact to a role, follow these steps:

 

  1. Right click on the name of a room and choose Manage access.

 

 

 
  1. Drag and drop a contact name into the Room Access window or click Close to cancel the operation.

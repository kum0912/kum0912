Organize SharedRooms

Once you have added room groups of shared rooms, you may find that you do not use all the rooms. Rooms can be organized for quick access.

 

You can mark your rooms in this room group as favorites by right clicking on the room and choosing Add room to favorites. This moves the room to the top of your list. You can also limit the rooms displayed by clicking the Menu icon and then ticking Only show favorite rooms.

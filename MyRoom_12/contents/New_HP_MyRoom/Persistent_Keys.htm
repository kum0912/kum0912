Persistent keys

Persistent keys are valid for your room until you revoke them. You can give out Persistent keys, either Host or Participant, to anyone to whom you do not wish to assign a role but you want to be able to access your room when they need to until you revoke the Persistent key. You need to have a paid Premium account to create Persistent keys for your rooms. Purchase the Premium account option by going to the MyRoom Products page.

 

Start setting up persistent keys by right clicking on the room name you will assign the keys to and choose Room keys, then Persistent.

   
You will also find the Persistent keys option in the Invite menu as in the example at right.

   
The first time you click on Persistent keys for a room, the message at right appears. Click on Create keys to create your first persistent keys for this room.

   
You have now generated both Host and Participant Persistent keys. Click on one of the three icons to the right of the required key to copy just the key, a full link in the form https://-www.myroom.hpe.com/attend/MRXXXXXXXXXXX or to email the link by launching your email application with the persistent link included in the body of the email . This is also the window you will see the next time you click on Persistent keys from the right click menu. You can revoke the current keys and generate a new set here, for instance in the event that  you feel more privacy is needed in your room, by clicking on the button.

   

You can use the first icon, Copy key to clipboard, by clicking it and then clicking the URL for HPE MyRoom at the bottom left of the window. This will open a browser window where you can click on the Join a meeting link in the top right of the browser window, paste the key into the Key field, enter your name, choose your language and click Join. click Download in the middle to download and install HPE MyRoom and then enter your copied key to attend your meeting.

 

As with joining a meeting directly from the HPE MyRoom log in page, a small install takes place and you are placed into the room. Your role in the room varies according to which type of key you have used to enter the room.

   
To use the Persistent key attend URL, open a browser and paste the link into the address field. The page opens with the key filled in. Enter your name, change your language if desired, and click Join. On the page that appears, click the Download link to install HPE MyRoom and then enter your key.

   

As with joining a meeting directly from the HPE MyRoom log in page, a small install takes place and you are placed into the room. Your role in the room varies according to which type of key you have used to enter the room.  

 

If someone enters with a participant key, a presenter can promote them to presenter by right clicking their name and choosing Make presenter or Make participant.

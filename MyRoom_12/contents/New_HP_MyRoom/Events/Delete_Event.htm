Delete an event  

 

To delete an event, right click on the name of the room in which the event is scheduled in the Rooms tab and choose Events or click on the room in the Rooms tab and click the Events button at the bottom of the tab.

 

 
The Events window opens with the name of the room you have chosen in the title bar. Right click on your event and choose Delete from the menu.

 

 
You can also highlight the event and choose the Delete option under the Events list.

 

 

Click Yes to confirm deletion of the event or No to keep the event as previously scheduled.

 

Note: there is no Undo function for this task, once deleted, the event and associated keys are gone.

Use Outlook to attend your event

You can enter your HPE MyRoom event from your Outlook meeting.

 

Enter your event   

 

At the appointed time of the meeting, or during the preconference time allowed (usually 30 minutes before the event), open your Outlook meeting. Click on the appropriate URL to start your meeting.  

 

You can also copy the appropriate key and use it to enter from the Tray or the Log In pages.     

On the Join page, enter your name in the name field. The key is automatically placed in the Key field. Choose your preferred language and click Join.

 

 
Click Run to install HPE MyRoom.

 

 
When the installation has finished, you are placed into your HPE MyRoom meeting.

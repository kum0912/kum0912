Sending event details

Once your event has been created, you can send the information to those you want to invite to attend. There are several ways to do this: copying the event details, emailing or creating a calendar item.

 

Copy event details  

 

Right click on the event in the Events window and choose Copy event details. The information is copied to your computer's clipboard.

 

 
Paste the details into an email, an instant message or a note and use them as needed to invite others to your scheduled event.

Click on a key for an event and choose Copy or Copy URL. Copy places just the selected key on the clipboard, Copy URL copies the complete clickable link to the clipboard. You can then send these in an email or instant message. Clicking the Email icon will launch your local email application with the complete information for the event in the body of the email.

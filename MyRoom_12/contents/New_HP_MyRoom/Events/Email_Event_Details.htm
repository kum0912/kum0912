Email event details

The Email option in the Events right click menu will open a new message in your local email application so that you can send an invitation for the event with all details included or edit it as you need to. This allows you to hold meetings with people who do not have HPE MyRoom accounts so that they can attend on a one-time basis. In addition, by choosing which key an attendee receives, you can limit their role inside your MyRoom during the meeting.

 

Email event details  

 

To create an email with the event details, right click on the name of a room in the Rooms tab and choose Events or click on the room in the Rooms tab and choose the Events button at the bottom of the tab.

 

 
The Events window opens with the name of the room you have chosen in the title bar. Right click on your event and choose Email from the menu.

 

 

Your email application opens a new message with the details of the event included in the body. If you want all invitees to only be participants in the meeting, delete any information about the presenter key, enter the email addresses in the "To" field and click Send. At right is a sample email using Windows Outlook.  

 

Note: A Test Your Setup link is added to the email at the bottom so attendees can prepare in advance of your meeting.

Attend an event - presenter

People attend events either as presenters or participants, using the key issued to them by the owner or scheduler of the HPE MyRoom. Presenters have control of all aspects of the event. Hosts in the room when the preconference time for an event starts are promoted to presenters with some limitations: see them here. There are three ways to attend an event or meeting as a presenter for those using Windows. Macintosh and Linux users can attend from a log in page or from their account.

 

 

Join a meeting from the home page  

 

As a Windows user, to join a meeting as a presenter, either copy the Presenter key sent to you by the room owner, go to the MyRoom home page, and paste the key into the Room Key field in the Join meeting section or click the URL link provided in an invitation to launch in a browser. Enter your name into the Name field (this is the name that will be seen by everyone in the event). Choose your in-room language and click on Join.

 

If you use the URL to attend your meeting and you have previously installed the MyRoom application "for everyone", you will be prompted to Open MyRoom.exe. Click the Open MyRoom.exe button to open the room.

 

For IE users, if you uncheck Always ask before opening this type of address, the next time you use the Attend URL you will be taken directly into the room without the pop-up.

 

For Edge, Chrome, Firefox and other browsers, if you check Always open these types of links in the associated app, the next time you use the Attend URL you will be taken directly into the room without the pop-up.

Macintosh and Linux users should click on the Download link in the Join meeting section, install HPE MyRoom and then launch the application to enter your key and join the meeting.

 

Be aware that by attending an event with HPE MyRoom, you are accepting the HPE Terms of Use. You can read the Terms of Use by clicking on the button provided.

 

 
If you try to enter the room more than 30 minutes before the event's Start Time, you will see the message at right.

 

 

As you enter, a small box appears to remind you that the Microphone is muted. You can click on Don't show this again to avoid this message in the future. NOTE: PulseAudio is required for Linux systems using MyRoom version 10.9 and higher.

 

The event opens displaying all attendees currently in the room. Presenters and those with a role in the room will display the Presenter icon on the far right in the People panel. Anyone who enters using the Participant key will not display an icon.

 

Find out about Roles here.

 

You can now import content, display it in the center stage area, share your desktop and in general, conduct a meeting or conference with your audience.

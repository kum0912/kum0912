Attend from tray - participant

People attend events either as presenters or participants, using the key issued to them by the owner or scheduler of the HPE MyRoom. Participants can chat, ask questions, and, when granted permissions by the presenter, use webcams or a microphone as well as the annotation tools.

 

Attend an event  

 

To attend an event as a participant using the tray, launch the application and log into your HPE MyRoom account. Copy the participant key sent to you by the room owner and paste the key into the Key field near the bottom of the window.  Click on the chevron to the right to enter the meeting.

Be aware that by attending an event with HPE MyRoom, you are accepting the HPE Terms of Use. You can read the Terms of Use by clicking on the button provided.

 

 
If you try to enter the room more than 30 minutes before the Start Time of the event, you will see the message at right.

 

 

The event opens with a blank page. As you enter, a small box appears to remind you that the Microphone, if the presenter has granted it, is muted. You can click on Don't show this again to avoid this message in the future. NOTE: PulseAudio is required for Linux systems using MyRoom version 10.9 and higher.

 

Click the See more icon to expand the choices. Click on the Show People panel icon to display all attendees currently in the room. presenters and those with a role in the room will display the Presenter icon on the far right in the People panel. Anyone who enters using the participant key will not display an icon.

  

Find out about Roles here.

  

You can now see content as the presenter displays it, export files from Handouts, as well as use audio and webcam and other tools assigned by the presenter so you can fully participate in the meeting.

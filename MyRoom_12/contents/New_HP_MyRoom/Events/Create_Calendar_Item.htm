Create calendar item

The Create calendar item option in the Events right click menu will open an appointment in your local email application. You can save the event to your calendar and when ready, send an invitation for the event with all details included or edit it as you need to. This allows you to hold meetings with people who do not have HPE MyRoom accounts so that they can attend on a one-time basis. In addition, by choosing which key an attendee receives, you can limit their role inside your MyRoom during the meeting.

 

Create calendar item   

 

To create a calender item with the event details, right click on the name of the room in which the event is scheduled in the Rooms tab and choose Events or click on the room in the Rooms tab and choose the Events button at the bottom of the tab.

 

 
The Events window opens with the name of the room you have chosen in the title bar. Right click on your event and choose Create calendar item from the menu.

 

 
Your calendar application opens a new appointment with the details of the event included in the body. If you want all attendees to only be participants in the meeting, delete any information about the Presenter key, enter the email addresses in the "To" field and click Send Update.

Rename a contact group

  1. Right click on the name of the group to be renamed.

  2. Click on the Rename contact group choice.

  1. Erase the old name of the group and type a new one. The name is restricted to 64 characters in length and cannot contain the character '&'.
  2. Click OK to complete renaming the group.

 

 
The group is now renamed and is placed in the list alphabetically.

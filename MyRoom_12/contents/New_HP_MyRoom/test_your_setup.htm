Test your setup

Windows users can check their ability to launch an HPE MyRoom session by running Test your setup. As a presenter, you can suggest your participants run Test your setup before a meeting to set up their audio and video for a meeting or training session.

 

Go to the HPE MyRoom home page and click on the Start button in the Test your setup section on the right side of the page.  

   
A short program will run to install MyRoom on your machine and the Test your setup room will then open. You may have to click Run to start the program's installation. Follow the directions on the screen to adjust your audio and video settings for your optimal use of HPE MyRoom in your meetings.

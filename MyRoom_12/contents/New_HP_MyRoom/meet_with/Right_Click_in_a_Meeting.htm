Right clicking on your own name gives different options. You can mark yourself as away in a meeting. Use this when you are away from your machine during a break or lunch. Changing this option does not affect your status in your HPE MyRoom tray, it is your status in the event.

Â 

As a presenter, you can also block private chat from anyone else in the room by choosing Block private chat. Use this when you want to minimize distractions during a presentation or training. When it is time for discussion or chatting, right click and untick Block private chat to allow incoming chats.

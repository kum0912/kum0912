Starting the phone bridge

Click on the Join audio bridge icon to start your phone bridge. Choose the entry name you wish to launch from the drop down list and click Make Call.

 

 
The phone bridge will launch so that your attendees can dial in on their telephones. Click on Hide in the Making Call window to minimize the number of windows you have open during your meeting or event. Once the meeting has ended, click on Hang Up to end your call.

 

 
While the call is ongoing, the icon changes to let you know the bridge is live.

 

 
To make any changes to your bridge entries, click on the Join audio bridge icon and choose Add/Edit My Audio Bridge entries.

 

 
You can now make manual changes to your phone bridge entries. Click on the box you want to change, make the edits and click Save and Close.

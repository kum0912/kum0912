Working with background images

When you click on an image in the Content panel, it loads to the center area as a background attached to a whiteboard. Although participants cannot see or select content items, they can use their mouse wheel or touchscreen to make their own image of the content larger or smaller. Please note that the explanations below apply when all users in a room are using the latest release.

 

Right click on any image content item loaded in the center area to display the menu at right. Using this menu, you can import images as Stamps, move them in front or behind each other, cut, copy and paste them, restore to an original size after resizing using your mouse wheel, delete the item, erase all annotations and attach or detach the image background.

   

When the content is attached to the background and a presenter changes their view of the content either by using Fit to page/Actual view or by scrolling with the mouse wheel or using a touchscreen, there is no change for any other viewer.

Stamps

 

As a presenter, you can drag and drop or import other images on top of your content item to create stamps. Right click on stamps and detached content items to move them in front or back, restore them to original size, erase annotations or, except for the original content item, delete them. If you try to delete the original image using Delete in the right click menu, you will see the message at right.

File transfer

Transfer files from one user to another. You can transfer up to 500MB of files at a time.

 

You can transfer files to a contact in a chat, meeting or event. Select their name in the People list, then choose the Actions menu and Transfer file.

   
If you have not selected a Contact, the error message at right will appear. Click OK to close the window.

   
Browse to the location of the file to send, select it and click Open to start the transfer.

   
You can also transfer files by right clicking on a name in the People panel in a chat or event and selecting Send file.

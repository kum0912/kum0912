Keyboard shortcuts

Below is a list of keyboard shortcuts for actions you may do regularly in HPE MyRoom.

 

Windows/Linux Mac Action
General    
Ctrl+Q (from tray) Command+Q Exit
Esc   Stop remote control during App Share
Shift+Esc   Stops sharing when sharing toolbar is in focus
Ctrl+A Control+A Toggle images between Actual Size and Fit to Page
Ctrl+P Command+P Print
Alt+F4 Command+W Close window in focus (room or tray)
Windows+F4 (Windows) Ctrl+F4 (Linux) Command+F4 (sometimes fn+Command+F4) Toggle microphone on and off
Ctrl+D Command+D Starts Desktop sharing
Ctrl+F4 (Windows) Ctrl+W (Linux) Control+W Close content item in center area, shows in File menu
     
Whiteboard Drawing Tools    
Ctrl+A Control+A Toggles between Actual Size and Fit to Page
Ctrl+H Control+Command+H Highlighter
Ctrl+R Control+Command+R Text
Ctrl+I Control+Command+I Pencil
Ctrl+O Control+Command+O Oval
Ctrl+F Control+Command+F Filled Oval
Ctrl+Q Control+Command+Q Square
Ctrl+B Control+Command+B Filled Square
Ctrl+L Control+Command+L Line
  Control+Command+C Choose Color
Ctrl+E Option+Command+E Erase all annotations
Key Up   Previous slide (room must be in focus)
Key Down   Next slide (room must be in focus)
     
Notes    
Ctrl+H Shift+Command+H Export as Html
Ctrl+Shift+T Shift+Command+T Export as txt
Ctrl+Shift+D Shift+Command+D Export as pdf
Ctrl+F4 Control+W Close notes in center area
Ctrl+Z Command+Z Undo
Shift+Ctrl+Z Shift+Command+Z Redo
Ctrl+X Command+X Cut
Ctrl+C Command+C Copy
Ctrl+V Command+V Paste
Ctrl+B Shift+Command+B Bold
Ctrl+I Shift+Command+I Italics
Ctrl+U Shift+Command+U Underline
Ctrl+L Shift+Command+L Align left
Ctrl+E Shift+Command+E Align center
Ctrl+R Shift+Command+R Align right
Ctrl+J Shift+Command+J Align justify
     
Chat    
Ctrl+X Command+X Cut
Ctrl+C Command+C Copy
Ctrl+V Command+V Paste
Ctrl-A Command+A Select all
     
Translation Services    
Ctrl+T Command+T Toggle Translate Chat on/off

Snap

Take a snapshot (still photo) of your own video stream or your application sharing.

 

After clicking the Snap icon (), the Snap option menu appears as shown at right. Choose Snap region to snap only one part of the desktop, including anywhere on the secondary monitor if present, Snap primary entire desktop, or Quick snap region.

   
Snapping the primary entire desktop immediately takes a snap of the full desktop, including the HPE MyRoom window, and places the snap in the content area where it can be selected for display in the Center panel, as at right.

   
Choosing Snap region brings up a Snap region window. Pull the corners or sides of the Snap region to change the size and area of the Snap region as desired. The region can be moved by clicking and dragging the Snap region title bar.

   
Once the desired area is selected, click on Snap in the title bar. If another snap is needed, move or size the region and then click Snap again. This allows you to take multiple snaps without opening the Snap area again. The snap is placed in the Content area where it can be selected for display in the Center panel. To end the Snap, click on the close button in the top right hand corner of the window.

   
Quick snap region lets you take one quick snap of a region you outline without the HPE MyRoom event or chat window in it. Once you choose Quick snap region, the HPE MyRoom windows disappears and the screen lightens. Holding down the left mouse key, use the crosshairs to outline the region you want to snap. As soon as you release the mouse key, your snap is placed in the Content area and displayed in the Center area.

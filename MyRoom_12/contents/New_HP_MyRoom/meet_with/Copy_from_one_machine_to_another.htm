Copy/paste from one machine to another

If you are using either MyRoom Assist or MyRoom University, text can be copied from one machine to another, in either direction, by the person controlling the share. The procedure is to copy on one machine to the clipboard, click the appropriate button on the share window and paste from the clipboard on the other machine.

 

Once the share has started, you will see three icons at the bottom of your screen.

   
To start the copy/paste, first place the text on the clipboard by right clicking and choosing Copy. In this case, the text is on the remote machine and will be copied to the local machine.

   
Click on the Send contents of remote clipboard to local clipboard icon at the bottom of the sharing window. The text is now on your local clipboard.

   
Navigate to the location where you want to place the text on your local machine and paste it there.

   
Your copy/paste is now complete. You can do the reverse, copy from your local machine and paste to the remote machine, by using the Send contents of local clipboard to remote clipboard button.

Back/Forward/Bookmark

These three options help you to move through your content items.

 

Back   

 

The Back option lets you choose non-sequential content items and reverse your steps, much like the back button in a browser. The Back option is visible once you have chosen your second content item.   

 

Right click in the content menu area and choose Back or use the keyboard shortcut Alt+Left to move back to the previous content item you had selected. Repeat up to 10 times. When you reach the first slide you had chosen in the content menu, the Back option will be grayed out.

 

Back and Forward save up to 10 items in their history.

   

Forward  

 

The Forward option lets you choose non-sequential content items and repeat your steps, much like the forward button in a browser. The Forward option is visible once you have used the Back option.    

 

Right click in the content menu area and choose Forward or use the keyboard shortcut Alt+Right to move forward to the next content item you want selected. Repeat up to 10 times. When you reach the last slide you had chosen in the content menu, the Forward option will be grayed out.

 

Back and Forward save up to 10 items in their history.

 

 

Bookmark  

 

You can place a bookmark to return to a certain content item rapidly. Only one bookmark can be placed per room and each bookmark is persistent until cleared. Folders cannot be bookmarked.  

 

Right click the item to be bookmarked and choose Bookmark. If Bookmark is grayed out, the content item type cannot be bookmarked, such as a folder.

 

 

Once you have set a bookmark on a content item, you can go directly to that item by either right clicking anywhere in the content area and choosing Go to Bookmark or using the  Alt+Home keyboard shortcut.  

 

To clear the bookmark in the room, right click anywhere in the content area and choose Clear Bookmark.

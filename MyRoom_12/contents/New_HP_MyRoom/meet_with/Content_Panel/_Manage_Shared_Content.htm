Import content

Many types of content can be imported into the content area of HPE MyRoom by presenters. PowerPoint and PDF files are individually converted to PNG files for display in MyRoom.

 

  • Windows imports and converts PowerPoint files, PDF files, JPG, BMP and GIF files in MyRoom. See Shared Content/Handouts.
  • Linux imports and converts PDF files, JPG, BMP and GIF files. PowerPoint files can be uploaded without conversion for sharing with participants. See Shared Content/Handouts.
  • Mac imports and converts PDF files, JPG, BMP and GIF files. PowerPoint files can be uploaded without conversion for sharing with participants. See Shared Content/Handouts.

 

Importing content   

 

You can drag and drop content from the desktop or a folder into the content panel. You can also drag an entire folder into the content panel.   

Mac users should note that since HPE MyRoom uses Microsoft Office Automation to convert PowerPoint to PNG files and Microsoft does not support Office Automation on Macs, you should first do the following:

 

  • Save your PowerPoint presentations using Save as Pictures, selecting PNG format, Save ALL.
  • A new folder will be created with your presentation name.
  • Drag the folder into your room and your files will be uploaded and available for your meeting.

 

Folders with content can be dragged from room to room, dragged from a desktop or imported. Individual content items can also be moved from room to room directly and dragged to a desktop or local folder. When the item is dragged to another room, any annotations are preserved: when dragged to the local drive, annotations are collapsed into the item.

   
Another way to bring content into a room is to right click in the content panel and choose Import. Browse to the location on your computer and click Open to start importing the file. Multiple files can be uploaded at the same time by selecting those you want to import.

 

 
When you move PPT or PDF content into a room, you see the message at right. This allows you to choose to upload just the original document in its original format, PPT or PDF, or to convert the document for display in MyRoom as you upload it and keep the original document as well. You can change these settings back in Settings/Options. Click OK after you tick the box or boxes you want or click Cancel to close the window.

 

 
After accepting the above message, a progress window opens displaying the upload status. The process can be stopped by clicking the Cancel button.

   

When the upload is complete, the file or folder is displayed in the content panel and can now be exported, renamed or deleted from the content panel by right clicking the file or folder name and choosing the desired action. PowerPoint and PDF original files can also be converted to HPE MyRoom content for use in the room by choosing the Convert option.  

 

Files and folders can be dragged up and down the content list and placed in different orders for clarity and ease of use.

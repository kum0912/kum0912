Survey

You can ask closed ended questions of the audience and they respond anonymously. Surveys are available as part of the HPE MyRoom University and are useful for training sessions and collaborative meetings. You can use surveys in HPE MyRoom to gauge interest level, involve participants and vary your content. Once created, a survey can be dragged from one room to another.

 

Create a Survey  

 

Right click in the Content area or click on the Open content file menu icon in the Content header. Click on Add and choose Survey.

 

 
  • Name your survey so that you can easily locate and start it in the Content area.
  • Enter the question you are asking with the survey.
  • You can use the Pre-defined Choices drop-down menu to quickly set up Yes/No, Agree/Disagree or Satisfaction Scale surveys.
  • Alternatively, you can create your own answers by typing in the spaces provided. NOTE: Character limits for the questions and answers are 320 characters including spaces for a Center survey and 100 characters including spaces for a Left survey.
  • Change the number of answers allowed in the drop-down menu, for instance if you want the top three choices from the list, choose 3.
  • Tick or untick the options below to define how your survey should function.
    • Unchecking Allow vote change will not allow your respondents to change their mind once a choice is made.
    • Always share results shares the results to your participants. Once the survey has run, another option allows you to share the results then if you choose.
    • Show counts displays the count of how many responses have been made.
    • If you tick Auto start, the survey will start as soon as you choose it in the Content panel.
    • Set to display it in either the Center area or the left panel. You can run one survey in the Center area while another runs in the left panel.
  • Click OK to save your survey.

 

 
Your survey now appears in the Content area with the title you gave it.

 

 
You can return to the Survey setup window by right clicking the survey you wish to change and choosing Properties. You can modify all options except the display location of the survey at this time. Click OK to confirm your changes.

 

 
When you are ready to run your survey, click on it in the Content area. If it is set to Auto start, it will automatically display and start running. If you did not choose this option, use the Start button in the top right hand corner to start your survey.

 

 
Once running, the Start button changes to a Stop button. You can also close the survey window using the icon on the right hand side . Presenters see the choices made regarding the setup of the survey through the text at the bottom of the survey.

 

 
If you have not shared the survey results, participants see this in the window. You can start sharing them by clicking the Share button .

 

 
When you share the survey results, this is what participants see.

 

 
You can clear the survey results, save them to your local machine or close using the buttons on the right of the survey window.

 

 

To see the survey results, click on the Help button and choose HPE MyRoom Portal from the menu. A browser window will launch to your My Account tab. Click on Rooms at the top to see your list of rooms. Click on Surveys next to the room in which you have run the survey and then the appropriate Survey Name and instance that you want to see. You will see the results for this instance of running your survey and you can download the results to run statistics for your surveys. Note that times are in UTC.  

 

To see all your different surveys in this room, follow the directions here.   Surveys can be dragged from one room to another.

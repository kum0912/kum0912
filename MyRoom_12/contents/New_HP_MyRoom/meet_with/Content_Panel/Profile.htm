Business card

You can use this as a presenter or instructor to tell your audience who you are.

 

 

Add your business card

 

If the Content panel is not displayed, click on the Show Content panel button.

 

 

Click on the Open content file menu button or right click in the Content panel, hover over Add and then choose Business card.

 

 

Your business card window opens.

 

You can tick Instructor or Presenter or type in the Heading field to define your role in the room. Type your name and title. If you want to place your picture in your business card, click the Browse button and browse to the location of your picture. Complete any other fields you want displayed in your business card and click OK.

 

Your business card is now in the Content list, with this icon and your name. Multiple business cards can be created so when you have different presenters in a room, each one can have a business card.

 

 

When you click on the Business card in the Content list, it becomes visible above the People panel to everyone . The Heading, Name and Title are displayed. This remains open as you navigate through other content items. Anyone can close the Business Card for everyone by clicking the X in the top right corner.

 

 
If the Business card button is clicked, the Business card window opens with the remainder of the information you have chosen to include. Click OK to close the window and, if you want, click the X in the main Business Card window to close that. Remember that closing the Business card above the People panel closes it for everyone else in the room.

Delete a content item

Delete a content Item if you no longer need it. For example, delete a Whiteboard you no longer need in your room.

 

If the content panel is not displayed, click on the Show content panel button.

 

 
Right click the content to be deleted in the content panel and choose Delete.

 

 
Click on Yes to confirm the deletion or No to cancel the action. NOTE: There is no Undo function after deleting content. Use with caution.

 

 
The content item is deleted from the content panel.

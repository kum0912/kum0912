Leave audio and video message

Audio and video messages can be recorded to send to any of your MyRoom contacts or leave as a message in a room. Messages can be sent to contacts at any time for delivery when the contact is online or if they are offline or away. If you are in a chat, the message is delivered to everyone in the chat because the file is placed in the chat area. Messages are limited to 60 seconds in duration and are recorded at a resolution of 320 x 240 pixels. This gives a smaller video with lower resolution but uses less bandwidth versus the recording of a meeting. NOTE: You cannot send a message to someone who is marked as unavailable or do not disturb.

   

To create and send a message to a Contact, follow these steps:

 

  1. Open a chat window with the contact by double clicking on their name in the Contacts tab of the main HPE MyRoom window.
  2. Right click in the content area to bring up the menu.
  3. Choose Add, then Message.

   
Or click on the Call button and choose Leave message.

   
The Recording window opens with Add audio ticked by default. If you want to make this a video message, check Add video as well.

   
  1. Click the Record button and start speaking to record the message. As soon as it is pressed, it toggles to a Pause button.
  2. Click the Pause button when you are finished speaking. The recording can be replayed for verification by clicking the Play button.

   
  1. Click Save when ready to send to the Contact or Cancel to quit without sending. Note that the Save button is only enabled if a message has been recorded. The message is sent as soon as you click Save.

 

If your microphone is not enabled in the Audio/Video section of the Settings option , you will not see any progress in the top bar or the large window. In that case, go to Settings to adjust your microphone.

Invite contact

Any presenter can invite someone in their Contacts list to join the room.

 

In the room, click on the Invite menu to expand it and choose Invite contact.

 

 
Your contact list opens. Highlight your contact's name and click Invite.

   

If the person your have chosen to invite to your room does not have a role in this room, you will see the window at right. You can select whether they enter the room as a participant or a presenter by clicking on the link.

 

If the person you invited is already an administrator, writer or reader for this room, you will not see the message.
   

If your contact is logged in to their MyRoom account and their status is either Available or Away, you will see the message at right until your contact responds.

 

You can also choose to send an email and mobile invitation to your contact or copy the invitation details to the clipboard. 

   
Your contact receives the message at right.

 

 
If your contact responds No, you will see the message at right. Click Close to close the window.

   
Once you have sent an email and mobile invitation, you will see this notification.

   
Your contact will receive an email with a link to enter the room and if notifications are turned on for MyRoom on their mobile device, the lock screen will display a message on their mobile device or if their application is open, they will see the second message.
   
When you copy the invitation to your clipboard, you will see the message at right.
 

 

If your contact responds Yes to the invitation, the contact is brought into the event in the role you have chosen, unless they already have a role in your room where they will enter as a presenter.  

 

You can repeat the steps above to invite another of your contacts or click Close to close the window.

Connection Status Details

The network metrics or connection status details are optional and turned off by default: they can be turned on by selecting the option Show network metrics in room status bar in the General tab of the Settings panel.

 
 

 
When the Room connection details section shows Room, as at right, it means that the two participants of a chat are connected through the HPE MyRoom servers. This happens when each is on dissimilar or firewalled networks. It will also show Room when there are more than two people in a meeting.

 

 
Two people inside the same network or on similar networks, for instance working within the same company, will be connected Peer to Peer for a slightly faster connection. This will be indicated in the Room connection details section as P2P, as at right.

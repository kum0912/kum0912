Use your account

Log in to your account at the MyRoom home page. You can click on My Account or Login to bring up the Login screen.    

   
Enter your email address (your account name) and click Next.

   

Enter your password and click Login.

 

If you forget your password, click the Forgot my password link to start the password reset process. Watch for an email from myroom@hpe.com with the password reset link.

 

After you have reset your password, enter your new password and complete the login process again.

   
Once logged in, you can click on My Account to see the list of products associated with your account, you can click on Home to see any organizations you belong to, or click on Rooms to see your list of Rooms.

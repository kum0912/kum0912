Purchasing HPE MyRoom Products

HPE MyRoom is available in a variety of offerings for purchase. See the list here for descriptions.

 

Go to the home page and click the Products tab. The list of available Products appears. Choose the product you want to purchase and click the appropriate orange button. You will first be prompted to login to your HPE MyRoom account. Follow the procedure for your chosen product to complete your purchase.   Here is an example of the Products page. HPE My Room products are continually updated, so check back regularly for new offerings.

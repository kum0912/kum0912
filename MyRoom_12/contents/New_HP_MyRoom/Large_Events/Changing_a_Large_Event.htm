Modifying a large event

Once a large event has been created, any of the parameters can be changed.

 

Modify a large event  

 

To modify a large event, right click on the name of a room in the Rooms tab and choose Events.

 

 

The Events window opens with the name of the room you have chosen in the title bar. Click on the name of your event.  

 

Change the details you need to change in the event: event capacity, start or end time or date, etc., and click Save to save the changes or Cancel to exit without changing. Note that if you ordered any services for the event, you must send an e-mail to hpmyroom.eventservices@hpe.com detailing the changes you make. Attach your emailed confirmation page to the e-mail.

 

 
Your event has been modified. The modified event details are shown in the window, including the Participant and Presenter keys, which are unchanged by the modification. These can be distributed through email or instant messaging through copy-paste or verbally over the telephone.

Enter a room

To enter a room from the Rooms tab, either double click on the room name or right click the room name and choose Enter.

 

 

The room opens for use.

 

 

There is one more way to enter any room in the Rooms tab (owned or assigned) - by selecting a room and clicking the Enter room button in the Key panel without creating a key. This method is only valid for your own rooms or rooms to which you have been assigned a role.

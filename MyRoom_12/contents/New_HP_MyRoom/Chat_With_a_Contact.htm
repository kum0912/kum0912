Ad hoc meetings (chat or pull a contact in)

You can have a quick impromptu chat or meeting with a contact. In addition, if you need to add someone to the chat, either of the first two people in the chat can "pull" someone else into the chat to join the conversation/extend the meeting.

 

Click to highlight a contact. Right click and click on the Chat menu option.  

 

You can also double click on the contact's name.

 

 
The Chat room opens immediately. When you type in the text area at the bottom of the window and press Enter, your contact is brought into the chat room. You can see this when the contact's name appears at the top or left hand of the window, depending on how many people are in the chat.

 

 

Once you are chatting with someone, you can pull another Contact into the conversation by drag and drop from the Contacts tab to the room, up to the maximum of your subscription.

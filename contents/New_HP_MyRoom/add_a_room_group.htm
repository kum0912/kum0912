Add a room group

You can organize rooms to which you have access by creating groups.

 

Add a room group  

 

Right click in the Rooms area and choose Add room group. You can also right click a room for a slightly different menu where you can also choose Add room group.

 

 
In the Add room group window that opens, type a room group name and click on OK to create the room or Cancel to stop the action. The room group name is limited to 64 characters long, including spaces, and cannot contain the character '&'.

 

 

The new room group now appears in the Rooms list.

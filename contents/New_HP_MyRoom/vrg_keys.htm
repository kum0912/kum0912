VRG keys

Choose the room you would like the VRG device to connect to when joining MyRoom. You must be an owner or administrator for the room in order to get the key. If you are not, then have the owner get a key for you. For most support scenarios, a Persistent Participant room key works best.  The key can be obtained from the MyRoom tray or from within the room itself.

 

From the MyRoom tray  

 

Right click on the room name and choose Room keys, then Persistent.

   

From within the room  

 

Click on the key icon on the toolbar, then choose Persistent keys as in the example at right.

   

Creating the keys  

 

The first time you click on Persistent keys for a room, the message at right appears. Click on Create keys to create your first persistent keys for this room.

   

You have now generated both Host and Participant Persistent keys. Click on one of the three icons to the right of the required key to copy just the key, a full link in the form https://-www.myroom.hpe.com/attend/MRXXXXXXXXXXX or to email the link by launching your email application with the persistent link included in the body of the email. This is also the window you will see the next time you click on Persistent keys from the right click menu. You can revoke the current keys and generate a new set here, for instance in the event that  you feel more privacy is needed in your room, by clicking on the Revoke Keys button.  

 

VRG Support  

 

For a VRG Support scenario, the participant key is the best choice. Click on the first icon next to the participant key to copy it to the clipboard.

   

You can use the first icon, Copy key to clipboard, by clicking it and then clicking the URL for HPE MyRoom at the bottom left of the window. This will open a browser window where you can click the Join a meeting link in the top right corner of the browser, paste the key into the Key field, enter your name, choose your language and click Join click Download on the right to download and install HPE MyRoom and then enter your copied key to attend your meeting.   

 

As with joining a meeting directly from the HPE MyRoom log in page, a small install takes place and your support client is placed into the room. The role in the room varies according to which type of key has been used to enter the room.

   
To use the Persistent key attend URL, have your support client open a browser and paste the link into the address field. The page opens with the key filled in. Have the client enter their name, change their language if desired, and click Join. On the page that appears, the client should click the Download link to install HPE MyRoom and then enter the key.

   

As with joining a meeting directly from the HPE MyRoom log in page, a small install takes place and your support client is placed into the room. The role in the room varies according to which type of key has been used to enter the room.  

 

If someone enters with a participant key, a presenter can promote them to presenter by right clicking their name and choosing Make presenter or Make participant.

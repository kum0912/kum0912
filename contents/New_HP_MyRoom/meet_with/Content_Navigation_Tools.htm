Content navigation tools

Clicking on a content item in the content panel opens that item in the large content area. The annotation tools appear when you click the See more icon above the content item. Presenters and those in a chat have access to all tools listed across the top of the content area. Annotation tools are available for participants if the presenter grants them. Click here to find out how. Click on hyperlinks below for additional help.

Content area menus

See more/See less

See more or less options available for use in the content area.

Previous icon

Displays the previous slide or object in the content area. Only visible once a content item has been selected. You can also use the left and up arrows on your keyboard to go to the last slide.

Next icon

Displays the next slide or object in the content area. Only visible once a content item has been selected. You can also use the right and down arrows on your keyboard to go to the next slide.

Close slide icon

Click to close the slide in the content area. The Getting Started page will appear or the Chat, if it is open below the content area.

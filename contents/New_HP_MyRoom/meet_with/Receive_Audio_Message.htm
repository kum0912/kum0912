Receive and play a message

You receive audio and video messages from a contact regardless of whether you are online (either available or away) or not. If you are online, the taskbar icon for HPE MyRoom will flash. If you are not online, any messages that have been sent  will be presented upon logging in to your account. NOTE: Messages cannot be sent to you if you are marked as unavailable or do not disturb. Messages appear in both the Chat area and the Content panel.

 

You play a message the same way, whether it is an audio message or a video message. Click on Play ()  to play the message or click on it in the Content panel. Click on Delete () to delete the message.

   

The Message window opens and the message starts to play only for the person who clicked Play.  

 

Click the Play/Pause toggle button to replay or pause the message, the Close button to close the message, or Delete to permanently delete the message from your chat or room.

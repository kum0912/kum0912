Microphone/Speaker/Video

Audio in HPE MyRoom is live audio streaming from a user's device which is broadcast to others in the room through their speakers (VoIP). Video in HPE MyRoom is live video streaming from a web or video camera which is broadcast to others in the room. Presenters have all three tools available to them in an event. Participants have only a speaker by default. They can be given access to the microphone and webcam tools through the Configure room for participants option in the Tools to manage people and participants button .

 

Microphone, Speaker and Video buttons at the top right of the MyRoom window allow control of these tools in a room.

   
By default, the speaker is on when you enter a room. Click the other icons to turn on those particular functions. Note the volume bars in the microphone and speaker icons that will show you when each is in use.

   
You can hover over the microphone and speaker icons to adjust the volume for each or change your video location using the webcam icon. To make adjustments to the microphone, speaker and webcam, click on the Settings icon and go to Audio/Video.

   

NOTE: MacOS has new security and privacy features that may affect your new installation of MyRoom on the Mac. If you experience difficulties with your microphone and camera such as microphone and camera not working for your test as described here, or if you have difficulty sharing your screen and you have answered Yes to any request to enable audio and video, follow these steps to enable audio and video and sharing for your Mac:

 

  1. Click on the Apple Menu.

  2. Choose System Preferences.

  3. Launch the Security & Privacy option.

  4. Click on the lock at the bottom to make changes.

  5. In the left column, click on Camera.

  6. In the right column, select HPE MyRoom to tick the box.

  7. If you have already launched MyRoom, you will be asked to quit HPE MyRoom to complete the access to your camera. Choose Later.

   

  1. In the left column, click on Microphone.

  2. In the right column, select HPE MyRoom to tick the box.

  3. If you have already launched MyRoom, you will be asked to quit HPE MyRoom to complete the access to your microphone. Choose Later.

   

  1. In the left column, click on Screen recording.

  2. In the right column, select HPE MyRoom to tick the box.

  3. If you have already launched MyRoom, you will be asked to quit HPE MyRoom to complete the process. Choose Quit Now.

  4. Launch HPE MyRoom.

 

NOTE: If any of the options are already checked, you may have to toggle them off and on again. Remember to log out of MyRoom to commit these changes.

Applaud

You can use the Applaud icon to indicate approval or agreement in the event.

 

In the Actions menu, you will find the Applaud choice. You can also find it next to the Raise hand button in the See more option..

   
Click this repeatedly to indicate agreement or applause in the room. The more you click, the more stars are generated and, if you have turned on the option in Settings/Options,  the more sound there is.

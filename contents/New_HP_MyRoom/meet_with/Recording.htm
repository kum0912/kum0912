Record your meeting

Records the center viewing area, videos, and application sharing, including both the auditory and visual aspects of the session. Recording is available in all offers except the 3 person Basic MyRoom. In the MyRoom 5 person offering, recordings are limited to 5 minutes in length. Any paid license has unlimited 2 hour recordings.

 

If you are a member of a chat or a room owner or presenter in an event in one of these offerings, you can record your meeting directly to your My Documents folder or to a default local or network location you specify in Settings/File handling for later playback. Everything in the Center area of the chat or event room is recorded unless you are sharing, along with the associated audio. Once you start sharing, it is the shared area, either region, desktop or application, that is recorded. Recordings are made at a resolution of 900 x 675 pixels using a 4:3 recording aspect. If you are going to use your recording for training or presentation purposes, we recommend sharing a region to maximize the view for the recording.

 

Recordings are now automatically saved in the MP4 format as of MyRoom 12.7. If you are on version 12.6 without the MP4 extension, you will not be able to add the extension and will be unable to record and will need to upgrade to version 12.7 to do so. If you have the MP4 extension on version 12.6 you will be able to record in MP4. If you then uninstall the extension, you will not be able to reinstall it, you would need to upgrade your version of MyRoom. 

 

Click on the Actions menu in an event and choose Recording controls or on the Show recording toolbar icon in the See more toolbar to launch the toolbar.

 

 
The Recording control box launches. You can start your speaker, microphone and webcam from this control box by clicking on each icon and then click on the Start button to start recording.

 

 

While you are recording,  you will see a small version of what is being recorded in the box and for everyone in the room, the Record button changes to a red box stating Recording.  

 

NOTE: As of version 10.10, the Annotation Tool bar is no longer recorded.

 

 
You can pause the recording, for instance during a break, by clicking the Pause button in the Record Control box.  

 

 

The Record Control window now shows recording paused. Start recording again by clicking the Start button.  

 

If you start sharing and another user starts a recording, you will see a small floating window with the name of the recording user on the machine that is sharing.

 

 
Click on the Stop button to finish recording.

 

 

Reminder:  

 

Use of this application is covered by the Terms of Use. The meeting you are attending may be recorded. Choosing the "Join" button gives your consent to such recording.

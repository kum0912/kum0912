Make presenter/Make participant/View profile

You can change the role of a person in the room from presenter to participant or participant to presenter.

 

You will see all the choices available in this menu if you have purchased MyRoom University. If you have the MyRoom Assist option along with MyRoom Premium or MyRoom University, you will see the Copy Clipboard, Get remote system information, Remote control, Tables and optionally, the Waiting room, options. Waiting room is only available in the MyRoom Assist option. Please go to the MyRoom Features page for a complete description of the features available in each option.

 

In a scheduled event, right click on the name of the participant to bring up the menu. Choose Make presenter to change the person from participant to presenter.

 

 
Repeat the process and choose Make participant to change the role from presenter to participant.

   

View profile  

 

In a scheduled event, right click on the name of the participant to bring up the menu. Choose View profile to see your contact's profile with the basic information of their name and email address and any other information they have added to their profile in Settings/Personal information.

Content panel

Content is files that are added to a virtual room and native MyRoom objects created in the room. Content is persistent in the chat or room until explicitly deleted. You can store up to 20 MB of content in your MyRoom Premium rooms. Upgraded versions of MyRoom have more storage capacity: see the Features page for details.

 

Use the Content panel to show or play content within the room, often for collaborative purposes. You can play Audio and Video recordings that have been created in MyRoom.  Many file types can be displayed in the viewing area: .png, .jpg, .bmp and .gif as well as PowerPoint presentations and PDF documents.  

 

Both PowerPoint and PDF files are converted to .png slides in HPE MyRoom under Windows. MyRoom for Linux and Mac can convert PDF files to .png for display in the room. Once converted, the resulting slides can be annotated like any other content.  Windows, Mac and Linux can also upload PowerPoint and PDF files without converting so you can use them in the Handouts folder for participants.

 

Once in the room, click on the Show Content panel icon to open the panel. To the right of the Content area heading is the Open content file menu button. Choose View to see the options for your Content panel.
   
Click on each choice at right to see the resulting changes to the view of the Content panel.    The bottom of the Content panel in this view shows the amount of storage used by content placed there.    HPE MyRoom Premium has a maximum of 20 MB of storage. More content is available in additional MyRoom products here.

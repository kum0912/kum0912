Invite by email

 

Any presenter can invite someone in their Contacts list to join the room through email.

 

In the room, click on the Invite menu to expand it and choose Invite by email.

 

 
The first time you invite someone by email, you will see the General page in Settings with the Invite by email section outlined in red. Choose your preferred method to send a MyRoom invitation.

 

 

Your email application will open with the Subject completed and text in the body of the email with the details of the invitation, including a clickable link. The invitation is valid for 15 minutes.  

 

Enter your contact's email address and send the email. Your contact can use the link to attend the event.

 

 

If you chose to use the MyRoom email service in the Settings/General window to send your email, you will see the window at right when you choose Invite by email. Enter email addresses separated by a space or a semicolon (;) and hit Send when you have finished.  

 

There is a limit of 100 email addresses that can be sent at one time.

 

 
You will see this message once your email invitation has been sent.

 

 

Quick key  

 

In the room, click on the Invite menu to expand it and choose Quick key or use the Quick key in the See more/See less menu. The 6-digit key will appear to the right of the event name and will remain visible and valid for 10 minutes. Should you need another one, you can repeat to generate another quick key.

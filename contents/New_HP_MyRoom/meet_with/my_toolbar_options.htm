My Toolbar options

You can choose which options you want displayed in your room, either chat or event. As each room is individual, the choices apply only to that room.

 

In a chat room, clicking on the My Toolbar Options on the right of the toolbar shows the menu at right. Ticking or unticking the options will display or hide that particular option on the toolbar.   Click Save to make your changes or Reset to Defaults to reset all the choices to the default presentation. Click Save after Reset to Defaults to make your changes.

   
In an event room, clicking on the My Toolbar Options on the right of the toolbar shows the menu at right. Ticking or unticking the options will display or hide that particular option on the toolbar.   Click Save to make your changes or Reset to Defaults to reset all the choices to the default presentation. Click Save after Reset to Defaults to make your changes.

View menu

The View menu lets you open the People panel, the Content panel or the Chat panel.

 

 

 

Icon or Area

Name

Comments

People panel

Click to open or close the People panel. See the People panel page for more details. By default in a chat with only two people, the People panel is closed.

Content panel

Click to open or close the Content panel. See the Content panel page for more details. By default in a chat, the Content panel is closed.

Chat panel

 

Click to open or close the Chat panel. See the Chat panel page for more details. By default in a chat, the Chat panel is open.

 

 

More information is available if the icon above is hyperlinked.

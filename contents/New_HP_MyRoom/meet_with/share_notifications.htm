Sharing notifications

You can share to others in the room:

  1. an entire desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux users),
  2. one monitor in a multiple monitor setup (Windows, Mac users),
  3. a region of your desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux users),
  4. or an application (Windows, Linux users).

 

Linux users cannot share a specific monitor and Mac users cannot share an application.

 

If you start sharing and another user starts a recording, you will see a small floating window with the name of the recording user on the machine that is sharing.

 

After you've shared to others in your room, a small button appears in the top right of their room with a popup indicating who is sharing.

 

   
If you close the popup and then hover over the button, you will see the title of the share window and the name of the person sharing.

 

 
If more than one person is sharing, the icon will show a small drop down arrow and display in the popup in the main room who is sharing. Each person sharing will see the same popup with information only about the other people sharing.  

   
Click on the small drop down arrow to choose which share window to bring into focus.

   
If you close the popup and then click on the button, you can bring your share windows into focus.

   
Choosing All windows or clicking the button will show all the share windows.

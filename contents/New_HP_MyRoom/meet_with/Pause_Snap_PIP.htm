Pause or Snap video

You can use webcams in HPE MyRoom to make your meeting or event more personal and interactive. Everyone who joins a chat controls their own webcam. Participants in an event have only a speaker by default. They can be given access to the microphone and webcam tools through the Control room features for participants option in the Tools to manage people and room features menu .

 

Turn on the webcam by clicking the icon in the top right corner.

   

Pause Video  

 

Click the Pause video icon under your video to stop broadcasting your own video.

   
Your video then freezes on the screen and the icon under it changes to a Resume video icon. Everyone else in the room sees "Paused" under your picture.

   

Snapshot your video  

 

Click the Snapshot your video to content icon under your video to take a snapshot of your video screen. The file uploads to the content area with the name format Snap Mmm-DD-YYYY.png.

 

NOTE: Only presenters can take a video snapshot.

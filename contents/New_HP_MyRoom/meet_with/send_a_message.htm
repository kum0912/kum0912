Working with breakouts

Presenters in the main room can send global or individual messages in breakout rooms, enter any breakout room or close individual or global breakout rooms.

 

As a presenter in the main room, you can enter any breakout room by right clicking the room name and choosing Enter breakout or by double clicking the breakout room name.

   
As a presenter, you can send a message to an individual room without entering it by right clicking the room and choosing Send message.

   
To send the same message to all rooms, right click on the word Breakouts to bring up the menu and choose Send message.

   
Those in the breakout room will see the message.

   
Any presenter in the main room can also end the breakout session individually or for all rooms. Right click the individual room or right click on the word Breakouts and choose End breakout or End breakouts respectively.

   
Everyone in the breakout room or rooms will be returned to the main event room and will see the message at right.

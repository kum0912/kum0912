About

In Mac, this option is available under the main HPE MyRoom menu, the first menu on the left of the top bar when MyRoom is selected.

 

The About box provides information regarding HPE MyRoom that may be needed by a Helpdesk Technician, such as the version number.  

 

When a newer version is available for MyRoom, you will see the Update now button to click. You can download and install the newer version. In addition, you can read about the improvements by clicking on the What's new link.  

 

When finished with the window, click OK to close it.

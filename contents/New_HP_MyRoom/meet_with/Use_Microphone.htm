Use microphone or video camera

You can selectively give the microphone or the video camera to any of your participants in an event. Useful when you want one person at a time to have the ability to use the devices.

 

You will see all the choices available in this menu if you have purchased MyRoom University. If you have the MyRoom Assist option along with MyRoom Premium or MyRoom University, you will see the Copy Clipboard, Get remote system information, Remote control, Tables and optionally, the Waiting room, options. Waiting room is only available in the MyRoom Assist option. Please go to the MyRoom Features page for a complete description of the features available in each option.

 

In a scheduled event, right click on the name of the participant to bring up the menu. Choose Use microphone or Use video camera and then Allow.

 

 
The People panel then shows the microphone or video camera icon next to the participant. The plus sign (+) indicates that the microphone or video camera is available and can be used.

   
You as the Presenter can then disallow the microphone for the participant by repeating the action and choosing Disallow.

   
The microphone or video camera then shows a minus sign (-) indicating that it is not available to the participant.

   

You can also use the Tools to manage people and room features button to  tick the Audio for all and/or Video for all selection boxes at the top of the people panel to quickly grant access for all your participants to control their own audio and video.  

 

Clicking on Default Permission removes the microphone or video camera icon  respectively from the participant and the People panel but the default permission, allowed or disallowed, remains for that user.

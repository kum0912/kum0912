Sharing desktop, region or application

You can share to others in the room:

  1. an entire desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux users),
  2. one monitor in a multiple monitor setup (Windows, Mac users),
  3. a region of your desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux users),
  4. or an application (Windows, Linux users).

 

Linux users cannot share a specific monitor and Mac users cannot share an application.

 

If you start sharing and another user starts a recording, you will see a small floating window with the name of the recording user on the machine that is sharing.

 

After clicking the Sharing icon (), the Sharing menu appears as shown at right.

 

 

If you have one monitor, the top choice will say Share entire desktop. If you have more than one monitor, the top choice is Share desktop with the option to choose which desktop you want to share.

 

 
Choosing Share Entire desktop immediately shares the primary full desktop with the HPE MyRoom window minimized and a small Share control window, as at right.  

   
For Windows and Mac users with more than one monitor, choosing Share and then Share desktop gives you the option to choose which monitor you want to share. The entire desktop is shared once you make your choice.

   
Choosing Share screen and then An application will display all open applications on your machine, for Windows and Linux users. Select the application you wish to share and just that application will be shared to your attendees.

   

Choosing Share region brings up a Share region window. Follow the directions in the window to pull the corners or sides to change the size and area of the region as desired. The region can be moved by clicking and dragging the Share region title bar.   

 

Once the desired area is selected, click on Start sharing in the Share control window. The shared region is seen in a green box.

   

Sharing can be paused by clicking on the Pause button in the Share control window and resumed by clicking the Start sharing button again. To end sharing completely, click the X in the top right corner or click on Stop in the Share control window.

 

Everyone in a private chat can share desktops or regions of desktops. Whatever is shared in the private chat is not seen by others in the main room, only those in the chat so you can privately discuss and show something to one person without the entire room seeing what is being shared.

 

 

There is a Snap button in the Share control window which will snap your shared area when clicked. Users with the Grant control product will see a fourth button in the Share control window . For more information, click here.

 

 

 

Ubuntu and Sharing desktop

 

Ubuntu 18.04 (LTE) resolves a previous Application Sharing issue.

 

Ubuntu 17.10 users will need to switch Ubuntu to use Xorg desktop which is available on 17.10 as part of the installation package. You can switch to the Xorg desktop by clicking on the gear icon near the Linux sing in button and choosing the correct desktop. If you have both Unity and Xorg, the dialog will show three (3) options.

   

NOTE: MacOS has new security and privacy features that may affect your new installation of MyRoom on the Mac. If you experience difficulties with your microphone and camera such as microphone and camera not working for your test as described here, or if you have difficulty sharing your screen and you have answered Yes to any request to enable audio and video, follow these steps to enable audio and video and sharing for your Mac:

  1. Click on the Apple Menu.

  2. Choose System Preferences.

  3. Launch the Security & Privacy option.

  4. Click on the lock at the bottom to make changes.

  5. In the left column, click on Camera.

  6. In the right column, select HPE MyRoom to tick the box.

  7. If you have already launched MyRoom, you will be asked to quit HPE MyRoom to complete the access to your camera. Choose Later.

   

  1. In the left column, click on Microphone.

  2. In the right column, select HPE MyRoom to tick the box.

  3. If you have already launched MyRoom, you will be asked to quit HPE MyRoom to complete the access to your microphone. Choose Later.

   

  1. In the left column, click on Screen recording.

  2. In the right column, select HPE MyRoom to tick the box.

  3. If you have already launched MyRoom, you will be asked to quit HPE MyRoom to complete the process. Choose Quit Now.

  4. Launch HPE MyRoom.

NOTE: If any of the options are already checked, you may have to toggle them off and on again. Remember to log out of MyRoom to commit these changes.

Actions menu - event

The Actions menu lets you raise your hand, applaud, snap a screen, share a screen, control your recordings, join an audio bridge and transfer files.

 

 

 

Icon or Area

Name

Comments

Raise hand

Click to raise your hand in the room. See the Raise hand page for more details.

Applaud

Click to applaud in the room. See the Applaud page for more details.

Snap screen

Click to open the Snap screen function. See the Snap page for more details.

Share screen

Click to open the Share screen function. See the Share page for more details.

Recording controls

Click to open the Recording controls window to start and control a recording. See the Recording controls page for more information.

Join audio bridge

Click to open the Join audio bridge window to control your audio bridge.

Transfer file

Click to open the File transfer function. See the File transfer page for more details.

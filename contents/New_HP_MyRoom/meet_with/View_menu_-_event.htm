View menu - event

The View menu lets you open the People panel, Content panel, Chat panel or the Question manager.

 

 

 

Icon or Area

Name

Comments

People panel

Click to open or close the People panel. See the People panel page for more details. In an event, the People panel opens in the left area.

Content panel

Click to open or close the Content panel. See the Content panel page for more details. By default in an event, the Content panel is closed.

Chat panel

 

Click to open or close the Chat panel. See the Chat panel page for more details. By default in an event, the Chat panel is closed.

Question manager

Click to open the Question manager. See the Question manager page for more details. By default in an event, the Question manager is closed.

Delete all room content

Delete all room content to clear up the content panel of your room. All content in the HPE MyRoom meeting can be deleted by any presenter, Owner, Administrator and Writer, except those who entered with a room key as Readers or those promoted from participant.

 

If the content panel is not displayed, click on the Show content panel button.

 

 
Right click the content panel  or click the Open content file menu in the content panel and choose Delete all room content.

 

 

Click on Yes to confirm the deletion or No to cancel the action. NOTE: There is no Undo function after deleting all room content. This operation deletes any recordings that are in the room as well. Use with caution.  

 

You can also choose to delete the chat history and question list as well by ticking the appropriate box.

 

 
All content is deleted from the content panel.

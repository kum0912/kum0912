Copy content

You can copy content to another location in the same MyRoom room or you can copy it between rooms. Any item including a folder can be copied and pasted.

 

If the content panel is not displayed, click on the Show content panel button from the View menu or by clicking the See more arrow.

 

 
Right click the content item you want to copy and choose Copy. You can copy any item in your content panel.

 

 
Go to the location, in the current room or another room, where you want to place the copied content and right click. The Paste option is now visible.

 

 

If you place your mouse on a folder in the content panel and right click, you will see the Paste After option. Choosing Paste After means that your copied item does not get pasted into the folder you have right clicked but right after it, allowing you to organize folders and content.

 

 
You can also drag and drop any content item from one room to another.

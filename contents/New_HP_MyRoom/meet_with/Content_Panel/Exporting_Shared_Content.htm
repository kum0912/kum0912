Export content - presenter

 

Content in the HPE MyRoom meeting can be downloaded by any presenter when the content panel is visible. Right click on the name of the file to be exported and choose Export from the menu.  

 

NOTE: PPT and PDF content folders can be exported as a unit. If you have used folders for organizing content in the HPE MyRoom, note that you must download other content items individually. See the New content folder page.

 

 
Navigate to the desired folder in the Save As window and click OpenSave. Your last save location is remembered.

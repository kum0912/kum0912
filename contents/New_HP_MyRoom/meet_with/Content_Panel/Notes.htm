Notes

A text editor with fixed and proportional fonts, bullets, bold, italic, underlined, and colored fonts. The Notes content item is a very useful tool for keeping track of ideas during discussions and can be used again after a meeting or event. Each note can be formatted in the meeting by any presenter and exported as HTML, plain text or as a PDF. Presenters can also move the Note into a Shared folder so participants can also export it.

 

Add a note

 

If the Content panel is not displayed, click on the Show content panel button.

 

 

Click on the Open content file menu button or right click in the content panel, hover over Add and then choose Notes.

 

 

A new notes page is created with the name listed in the content panel in the format Notes Mon-DD-YYYY.

 

 

When you click on the note in the content list, it becomes visible to everyone. The Notes Menu bar and the Notes Formatting bar become visible to everyone except participants.

 

 

Notes roles and permissions   

 

Based on roles and permissions within a room or an event, as defined in the grids at right, users can add or delete a note.

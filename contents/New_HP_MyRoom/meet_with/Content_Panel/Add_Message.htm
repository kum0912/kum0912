Message

You can use this as a presenter or instructor to leave a message, audio or video, in your room.

 

Add a message

 

If the content panel is not displayed, click on the Show content panel button.

 

 

Click on the Open content file menu button or right click in the content panel, hover over Add and then choose Message.

 

 
The Recording window opens with Add Audio ticked by default. If you want to make this a video message, check Add Video as well.

   
  1. Click the Record button and start speaking to record the message. As soon as it is pressed, it toggles to a Pause button.
  2. Click the Pause button when you are finished speaking. The recording can be replayed for verification by clicking the Play button.

   
  1. Click Save when ready to send to the Contact or Cancel to quit without sending. Note that the Save button is only enabled if a message has been recorded. The message is sent as soon as you click Save.

 

If your microphone is not enabled in the Audio/Video section of the Settings option , you will not see any progress in the top bar or the large window. In that case, go to Settings to adjust your microphone.

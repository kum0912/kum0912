Stamps

Stamps are a convenient way to highlight and manipulate pictures in HPE MyRoom to add extra interest.  

 

Create a stamp  

 

Any picture format that you drag and drop straight onto a Content item becomes a stamp. You can drag it from your desktop straight to the Center area.

 

 

You can also drag the content item from the Content panel.

 

 
Stamps can be resized and moved using your mouse. If you have a touchscreen, you can manipulate each stamp, pinch them, enlarge them, manipulate corners, etc.

 

 
Right click on a stamp to get the stamp menu. You can move one stamp in front of or behind another, restore them to their original size, cut, copy and paste, remove scale and rotation, delete one or all annotations.

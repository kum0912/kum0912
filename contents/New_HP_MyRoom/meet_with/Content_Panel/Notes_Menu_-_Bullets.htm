Notes menu - Bullets

The Bullets menu is used to format text with bullets.

 

The Notes menus appear for everyone except participants as soon as a note is selected in the content panel.

 

 
Bullets menu

 

 

Bullets are applied in one of two ways. In the first method, you can either change to a bulleted style on a new line before typing and the same bullet style will apply until you change it again or you can highlight the text you want bulleted, then choose Bullets and the style of bullet you want to apply.

 

 
After highlighting and selecting a bullet style, the bullet changes to what you have picked.

Customization

Customize the look of your HPE MyRoom rooms. Please note that if you are part of an organization that has established a default background, you cannot apply a theme.

 

Choose a theme to use in your rooms. The HPE globe is the default room theme.  

 

Untick or tick to apply the theme to your MyRoom tray as well. The default theme for your tray is white.  

 

Click on Done to save any changes.  

 

Close the window to stop making changes.

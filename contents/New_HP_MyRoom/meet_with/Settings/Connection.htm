Connection

If you use a firewall or proxy, enter a user name and password in the Connection window. In that case, untick the box next to Let MyRoom find its own connection method and additional information will be requested.  

 

Click on the option at right to see the expanded details required.  

Click on Save to save any changes.  

 

Close the window to stop making changes.

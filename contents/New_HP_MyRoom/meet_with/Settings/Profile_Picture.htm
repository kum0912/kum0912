General

Choose how you want MyRoom to behave when you launch Windows and MyRoom. You can also choose to display your network metrics in your room, show your video button before you make a call to a contact, display popup videos on a second display if you have one and how you want to invite users by email. 

 

You can also adjust the delay for moving your Status to 'Away' when your computer is inactive.  

 

Click Save to save your changes or Undo changes if you want to undo them.

 

Click the red X to close the window.

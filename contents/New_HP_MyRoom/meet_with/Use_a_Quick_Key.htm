Use a Quick key

Another way to get another person into a room is to create a Quick key for them. This is especially useful if the person is not yet a Contact but does already have HPE MyRoom installed. When on a teleconference, you can create a 6-digit room key and give this to all users for access to the room. Quick keys are valid for entry into the room for 10 minutes, after which you can generate another key.

 

Create a quick key  

 

In the See more menu, click the Quick key icon in your event.

 

 
A 6-digit number appears next to the event name at the top of the room. It is also placed in the Room key field in your tray.

 

 
Other users can then enter the quick key into the corresponding space in their MyRoom tray and click the Enter room button to launch and enter the room.

 

 
Note that Quick keys are only valid for 10 minutes. After that, another Quick key can be generated to bring others into the room.

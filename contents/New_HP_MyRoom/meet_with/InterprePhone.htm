IntelliConference Bridge

The phone bridge allows those who cannot use MyRoom audio to dial in on a telephone and attend a meeting. It is available as a paid add on through HPE MyRoom Products.

 

After choosing the IntelliConference bridge option, enter the Conference Room number and your language code from the drop down list.   Click on Save and Test.

 

 
Once your test call is answered, follow the voice prompts to complete your setup. Click Hang Up to finish, as in the example at right.

 

 
The next time you need to use the same IntelliConference bridge number, click once again on the Audio Bridge icon, choose the correct IntelliConference entry from the drop down list and choose Make Call .

   
To edit entries, click on the Join Audio Bridge icon and choose Add/Edit My Audio Bridge Entries. By selecting your bridge, you can modify the prompts to remove the extra pauses, P. You only need one P for every prompt. When done, click on Save and then Close to close the window.

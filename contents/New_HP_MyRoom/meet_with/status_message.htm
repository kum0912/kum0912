Room message

Use the Room message space at the bottom of your event window to place persistent messages such as your audio bridge number. The Room message has a limit of two lines with a total of 150 characters. The message is persistent in the room until you clear or replace it.

 

Click on the MyRoom menu at the top of the room and then Room message.

   

The Room message window appears. Type in the information you want to show in the room and click OK.  

 

Click on Clear to clear any current message in the box.  

 

Click Cancel to exit the box.

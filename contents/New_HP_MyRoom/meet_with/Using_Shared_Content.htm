Using content

Content is files that are added to a virtual room and native MyRoom objects created in the room. Content is persistent in the chat or room until explicitly deleted. You can store up to 20 MB of content in your MyRoom Premium rooms. Upgraded versions of MyRoom have more storage capacity: see the Features page for details.

 

Use the Content panel to show or play content within the room, often for collaborative purposes. You can play audio and video recordings that have been created in MyRoom.  Many file types can be displayed in the viewing area: .png, .jpg, .bmp and .gif.  

 

Many types of content can be imported into the content area of HPE MyRoom by presenters. PowerPoint and PDF files are individually converted to PNG files for display in MyRoom.

 

  • Windows imports and converts PowerPoint files, PDF files, JPG, BMP and GIF files in MyRoom. See Shared Content/Handouts.
  • Linux imports and converts PDF files, JPG, BMP and GIF files. PowerPoint files can be uploaded without conversion for sharing with participants. See Shared Content/Handouts.
  • Mac imports and converts PDF files, JPG, BMP and GIF files. PowerPoint files can be uploaded without conversion for sharing with participants. See Shared Content/Handouts.

 

Once in the room, click on the Show Content panel icon to open the panel. To the right of the Content area heading is the Open content file menu button. Choose View to see the options for your Content panel.

   

Click on each choice in the example at right to see examples of the resulting changes to the view of the Content panel.  

 

The bottom of the Content panel in this view shows the amount of storage used by content placed there.  

 

The limits for storage in HPE MyRoom are as follows: HPE MyRoom Basic has a maximum of 10MB of persistent storage, HPE MyRoom Premium has a maximum of 20 MB and HPE MyRoom University 150MB.

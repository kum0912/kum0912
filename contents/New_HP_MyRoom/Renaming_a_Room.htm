Rename a room

To rename a room, right click on the room name and choose Rename.

 

 

Erase the old name using the Backspace key or highlight and click the Delete key. Type in the new name of the room and click Save to confirm the new name or Cancel to stop the action.    

 

Name restrictions  

 

The room name is limited to 64 characters long, including spaces, and cannot contain the character '&'.   MyRoom administrators who are creating rooms for use with voice-controlled devices should avoid using symbols and special characters when creating room names.

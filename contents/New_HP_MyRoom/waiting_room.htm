Waiting room

Those with HPE MyRoom Assist and HPE MyRoom University can use a Waiting room to control movement of their participants. This is very useful for technical support. In a support environment where the technician/SME takes care of the next client in line, the client receives the participant key, either a Quick key or a Persistent key, for access to the waiting room. When the SME is ready to work with the next client, the SME moves the client from the waiting room to the SME's room: the client is simultaneously removed from the waiting room. Waiting room is part of the HPE MyRoom Assist and the HPE MyRoom University add-ons you can purchase here.

 

Create a room that will act as your waiting room by clicking the Add new room icon or by right clicking in the Rooms tab and clicking Add room. Name your waiting room and click OK to finish adding a new room.  

 

See here for more information.  

 

 

It is a good idea to remove several options from the participant view in the room by clicking on Tools to manage people and room features   and then Configure room for participants and then untick the People, Room chat, Private chat and Add questions options. Click Apply and then Close to apply your changes.  

 

Once this is done, participants in the waiting room cannot see who else is in the waiting room with them nor their position in line. You as the SME can still start a private chat with the client in the waiting room to advise them you are moving them to a new room.  

 

After these options have been removed once, they remain invisible to participants until the Restore defaults option is used or options are selected one by one.  

 

See here for more information.

 

 

Set up a Persistent Participant key for your waiting room by right clicking on your waiting room name and selecting Room keys and then Persistent. Set up a Persistent Participant key to provide to your clients.  

 

See here for more information.

   

Provide any new client with the Persistent Participant key or the URL containing the key.  

 

The client enters the waiting room as a participant, without the People panel, Room chat, Private chat or the Question manager, if you have removed those options as above.

   
When you are ready to work with the client, right click on the client's name in the waiting room, select Move to room... and then select the room where you want to move the client and yourself.

   
The client is removed from the waiting room and moved to the support room. You are placed in the support room if you are not already in the room but you remain in the waiting room as well.

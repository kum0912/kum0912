Granting access to SharedRooms

HPE MyRoom SharedRooms are a special permission not available to everyone. Once you have set up your process mailbox HPE MyRoom account, created the required rooms and grouped them, you can generate a provisioning key which you will give to those who need it. This grants them access to all the rooms in one group. Each key, once used by those who are given it, allows them to access all the rooms in that particular group as the room group will appear in their rooms list.

 

Setting up your provisioning key

 

  • Right click on the group name.

  • Select Manage group access.

   

Choose your provisioning key duration

The key duration is how long the key is valid. It does not indicate how long a user can have access.  

 

When a key has expired, no users can use that key to add that group to their list of rooms.

 

  • The first time you generate a key or if you have revoked and not regenerated a key, you will see a message that no valid provisioning keys are available.
  • Choose a key duration from the drop down list: 24 hours, 48 hours, 1 week, 1 month, 1 year, indefinitely.
  • Click Create Key to complete the operation or Cancel to stop the process.

   

Your provisioning key is now set.  

 

You are now ready to send your key to those to whom you want to grant access. Select the type of key you want to provide. You can either click the Copy icon to place the key onto your clipboard ready to be pasted into an email or click on Email to open a new email with the details of this key and a link in the body of the email, ready for you to add names to the TO: field and send the email out.  

 

Click Close to close the window.  

 

You can see the definition of the various roles here.

 

 

Revoking a provisioning key  

 

If you find out that a key has been wrongly used, revoke the key. Revoking the provisioning key will NOT remove any user who had already added the rooms to their account. Revoking the provisioning key will only stop new users from adding the room to their account.

 

  • Right mouse click on the room group.
  • Select Manage group access.
  • Select Revoke Keys.
  • Confirm that you want to revoke the keys.
  • Click Close to close the window.

 

NOTE: Once a key is revoked, it can no longer be used. It cannot be retrieved so be sure you want to revoke the provisioning key.

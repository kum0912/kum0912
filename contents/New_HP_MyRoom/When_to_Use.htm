When to use chat, meetings or events

HPE MyRoom can be used for different types of interactions. Here is a general guideline of when to use the different functions.

 

Chat
  • When you want to communicate with a contact on your list on a quick, one-to-one basis.
  • You can always drag another contact into your existing chat if needed so that more people are added to the conversation.
Meetings
  • When you have regular meetings with a group of your contacts. A dedicated room can be set up for meetings that occur regularly by assigning contacts access to your room. Grant each person who will attend the meeting a Role for the room, either Reader, Writer or Room Administrator. Your contacts will then see the room in their Rooms tab and can simply double click on it to enter the room and attend the meeting, even if it is a last-minute occurrence.
  • Additional attendees who do not have a role can be given a Quick Key to attend these meetings, up to the total capacity of the room.
Events
  • When you want to invite others not in your contacts list to attend, including people who are not yet HPE MyRoom users.
  • When you want an event to have a defined start and end time and a defined maximum capacity, depending on your version of MyRoom.
  • When you want event presenters to control the features given to participants.

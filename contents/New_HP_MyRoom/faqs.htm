Frequently Asked Questions

How much does HPE MyRoom cost?

 

  • HPE MyRoom offers a variety of products for web collaboration, delivering training, and remote support. You can view all the products on HPE MyRoom’s product page  along with the associated prices.

 

How much content storage is available with HPE MyRoom?

 

  • Content storage is determined by the HPE MyRoom product you have purchased. HPE MyRoom Features page lists the storage associated with the various products.

 

How do I determine the right HPE MyRoom account size for my needs?

 

  • You need to purchase the HPE MyRoom product that is the same or larger than your largest meeting or training you plan to conduct.  Each product offering is for a single account for one year.

How do I manage rooms and events?

 

  • HPE MyRoom Rooms shows you how to create, rename and delete rooms as well as how to manage access to your rooms by others. Go to Rooms or view the recording for Rooms under the HPE MyRoom Training area on the MyRoom Support page.
  • HPE MyRoom Events shows you how and why to schedule events, distribute event logistics, join an event, control participant features and use handouts. Go to  Events or view the recording for events under the HPE MyRoom Training area on the MyRoom Support page.
  • In addition, HPE MyRoom Add-in for Outlook 2010/2013 is available for Windows users to schedule events directly from Outlook. Please go to the MyRoom Outlook add-in page to download and install the add-in.

 

How do I have an always on room, available 24 X 7?

 

  • If you have a dedicated team of users who always use the same room, you should use HPE MyRoom Manage Access.  This is the MOST secure way to control who has access to your room.  To learn more about managing room access, please go to Roles.
  • If you have a room that others outside your immediate team need to access regularly and access can be at any time of day, then you would want to create a persistent key, which is available 24 X 7, and make it available to the users.  

NOTE:  Since this is a persistent key and could get compromised by someone accidently passing it along to someone unintended, it is the least secure and you should change the persistent key regularly here.

 

I deliver training to customers, partners and/or employees, I need surveys, timers, and breakout rooms. Which product should I purchase?

 

  • Use one of the HPE MyRoom University offerings, depending on your class size, which includes HPE MyRoom Premium plus additional features and functions requested by trainers such as surveys, timers, remote control, breakout rooms and persistent keys..

 

Can I record my sessions?

 

  • Yes, all MyRoom Premium, MyRoom University and MyRoom Assist products have unlimited 2 hour local recording capability.  MyRoom Basic has unlimited 5 minute local recording capability.

 

When I record, the recording is in WebM format.  I am used to WMV or MP4.  What is this new format?

 

  • Recordings are automatically saved in the WEBM format. If you want to save your recordings in MP4 format, install the MP4Recorder extension following the directions on the Settings/Extension page. This option is available for Windows 64-bit operating systems only at this time.

  • WebM was built for the web. By testing hundreds of thousands of videos with widely varying characteristics, we found that the VP8 video codec delivers high-quality video while efficiently adapting to varying processing and bandwidth conditions across a broad range of devices. VP8’s highly efficient bandwidth usage and lower storage requirements can help publishers recognize immediate cost savings. Also, the relative simplicity of VP8 makes it easy to integrate into existing environments and requires comparatively little manual tuning in the encoder to produce high-quality results. WebM video plays directly in your web browser using HTML5. No plug-ins are needed, but you must install a modern web browser that supports HTML5 and WebM.

I have HPE MyRoom installed, but every time I join a meeting using the link in the invitation I get another file installed.  Why?

 

  • The process to launch HPE MyRoom client from the web (either web page or link), when a user enters a key, requires downloading and running a special executable file called ‘bootstrap’ that actually launches MyRoom.

 

Sometimes my audio device or camera does not work, what troubleshooting steps can I take to resolve the issue?

 

  • Try your audio or camera device again after each step*
  1. Click the gear icon at lower right to open HPE MyRoom Settings
  • Close MyRoom Settings window
  • Sometimes just opening and closing HPE MyRoom settings resolves the issue
  1. Open HPE MyRoom Settings window, again
  • Select the device from dropdown that you want to use
  • Adjust microphone volume using slider
  • Adjust speaker volume using slider
  1. Unplug and then plug the audio device or camera from computer
  2. Test the audio device or camera in another application Install the device driver from manufacturer’s website
  • If the device does not work in the other application, the problem is with the device. Try another webcam or microphone
  1. Install the device driver from manufacturer's website
  2. Exit HPE MyRoom (chat or room) and enter it again
  3. Log out and then log in to your HPE MyRoom account (if you are logged into your account)
  4. Reboot your computer

*For Win 7 and Win 8, verify the following: In Control Panel/Sound/Communications tab, ensure that Do Nothing is selected so that volume is not reduced by the operating system.

How do I fix the error message saying the program cannot start because MFPlat.DLL is missing?

If you receive this error, please go to Microsoft to install the Media Feature Pack for your version of Windows. Some Windows versions do not by design have a Media Feature Pack so you will need to install it to run MyRoom.

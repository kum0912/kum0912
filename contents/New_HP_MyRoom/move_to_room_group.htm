Move to room group

After creating your room groups, organize your rooms by groups.

 

Right click on a room name and choose Move to room group or drag and drop the room name to the new Room Group name to move your rooms into the group.

   
Your rooms are now organized.

   
To remove a room from a group, right click the room name, choose Move to room group and then un-grouped.

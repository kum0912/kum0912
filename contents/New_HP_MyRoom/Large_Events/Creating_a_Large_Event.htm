Creating a large event

You can create and hold dedicated scheduled large events with up to 500 presenters and participants in HPE MyRoom. This allows you to hold meetings with people who do not have HPE MyRoom accounts so that they can attend on a one-time basis as well as those who already have MyRoom accounts. In addition, by choosing which key an attendee receives, you can limit their role inside your MyRoom during the meeting. The Large Event option is available as an additional Product for HPE MyRoom: add your Product Key at www.myroom.hpe.com.

 

 

Create a large event  

 

To create a new large event, right click on the name of a room in the Rooms tab and choose Events.

 

 
The Events window opens with the name of the room you have chosen in the title bar. Navigate to the date for your event and click on Create Event to start the process.

 

 
Enter the details for your event. Change the name of the event or accept the default name entered. Change the start and end times of the event,  and enter any teleconference information you need for your event.

 

Enter the total number of presenters and participants you will invite in the Capacity field. If you will be using MyRoom Event Services for the event, make sure to add one seat if requesting a facilitator, one seat if requesting a transcriber and one seat if requesting a recording technician. See the next page for details regarding Event Services. Click Save to schedule the event. Note that the event capacity is dependent on the owner's room, not that of any administrator that might be scheduling for someone else's room.

 

 

 
Your event has been created. The event details are shown in the window, including the Participant and Presenter keys. These can be distributed through email or your usual invitation method.

Managing large events

Here are some best practices when running a large event.

 

Before your meeting

 

  • Include a message in your meeting invitation recommending that all attendees use a headset plugged directly into your PC or a powered USB port on your docking station.
  • Recommend in your invitation that all attendees use Test Your Setup from the link at the MyRoom home page prior to start time.

 
 

 

Change participant settings  

 

Use the Tools to manage people and room features/Configure room for participants to restrict the options for your participants. For instance, you might not want your participants to see the People panel, be able to chat, click on Applause, have use of the Microphone, Speakers and Video Camera. Untick the options you do not want your participants to see or use and click Apply.

 

 

Change your personal options in MyRoom Settings  

 

Go to Settings and untick any options already selected that might distract you while you are presenting. The following are a few examples:

  • In the Notifications tab, in the section When someone enters the room, untick all the options so you are not disturbed by the windows popping up or the sound.
  • In the Notifications tab, in the section When a chat message arrives, untick all the options to minimize distractions. Remember to turn it on when chat time arrives.
  • If Chat will be available to participants, consider disabling Play sound when chat message arrives so that you don't hear constant pinging while you are presenting.
  • Disable Popup message and Play sound under When a contact comes online.

 

 

Restrict participant microphone use to question time  

 

If you want your participants to ask questions verbally during a question period, when you are ready to take the questions, tick the Microphone box to give the participants access to their microphones in the room. Participants can then unmute their microphones themselves to talk in the room.  

 

Another way to control microphone use is to enable or disable individual participant microphones as needed by right clicking on the participant's name and choosing Use microphone/Allow or Disallow.

 

 

Launch your audio bridge ahead of time  

 

If you are using an audio bridge for your meeting, open the audio bridge before start time to ensure your participants can join. Click the Join audio bridge button, select your entry and click Make Call.  

 

To set up your Audio Bridge Entries, follow the directions here.  

 

 

Request Audio/Video Specialist event service for your event  

 

An AV Specialist provides HPE MyRoom AV support for the participants, allowing presenters to focus on the presentation. You can order Event Services when setting up your large event.

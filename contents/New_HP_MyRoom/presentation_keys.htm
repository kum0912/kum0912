Presentation keys

Presentation keys are valid for a specific period of time for your room. You can give out Presentation keys, either Host or Participant, to anyone to whom you do not wish to assign a role but you want to be able to access your room for a specific period of time starting immediately; for instance, participants in an all-day training session .

 

Start setting up Presentation keys by right clicking on the room name you will assign the keys to and choose Room keys, then Presentation.

   
You will also find the Presentation keys option in the Invite menu as in the example at right.

   
When you click on Presentation keys for the first time or if  you have revoked previous keys for a room, the window at right appears. Click the drop down arrow and choose the length of time for which the keys should be valid. Click on Create keys to create your presentation keys for this room.

   

You have now generated both Host and Participant Presentation keys. Click on one of the three icons to the right of the required key to copy just the key, a full link in the form https://-www.myroom.hpe.com/attend/HXXXXXXXX or  https://-www.myroom.hpe.com/attend/AXXXXXXXX or to email the link by launching your email application with the presentation link included in the body of the email.  

 

This is also the window you will see the next time you click on Presentation keys from the right click menu during the validity of these keys. You can revoke the current keys and generate a new set here, for instance in the event that  you feel more privacy is needed in your room, by clicking on the button.

   

You can use the first icon, Copy key to clipboard, by clicking it and then clicking the URL for HPE MyRoom at the bottom left of the window. This will open a browser window where you can click on Join meeting in the top right of the window, paste the key into the Key field, enter your name, choose your language and click Join click Download in the window that appears to download and install HPE MyRoom and then enter your copied key to attend your meeting.  

 

As with joining a meeting directly from the HPE MyRoom log in page, a small install takes place and you are placed into the room. Your role in the room varies according to which type of key you have used to enter the room.

 

 

To use the Presentation key attend URL, open a browser and paste the link into the address field. The page opens with the key filled in. Enter your name, change your language if desired, and click Join. On the page that appears, click the Download link to install HPE MyRoom and then enter your key.

 

As with joining a meeting directly from the HPE MyRoom log in page, a small install takes place and you are placed into the room. Your role in the room varies according to which type of key you have used to enter the room.

Users of SharedRooms

Once a Provisioning key has been created, it will be sent to you as a user who needs access to the shared rooms.

 

  • You will receive an email with the room group provisioning key.

  • Paste the key into your HPE MyRoom tray.

   
All the rooms in that group will show up on your MyRoom tray along with a message of how many rooms have been added.

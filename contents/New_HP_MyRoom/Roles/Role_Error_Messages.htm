Changing roles

The Reader and Writer roles can work with content only. If a reader or writer tries to bring another contact into the room, the message at right appears. Click on OK to close the message.

 

 

 

Once the offer has been sent to the contact, the room owner can change the contact's role at any time by opening the Room access window and clicking on the name of the contact in the list, then choosing a different role and clicking Save and then Close to close the window.

 

 

Role access can be removed from a contact by the room owner by highlighting the contact in the room access window and clicking Delete. When the confirm dialog box opens, click Yes to confirm the deletion or No to cancel the operation.

 

 

 

 

A contact can remove an assigned room by right-clicking the room name and choosing Release room access.

 

 

 

 
Confirm the release of access to the room by clicking OK or cancel the operation by clicking Cancel.

Accepting a role

If the contact is logged in, a message such as the one at right appears immediately, if not, it appears at the next log in to HPE MyRoom.

 

 

 

When the contact accepts the offer, the room owner sees the status as Accepted.

 

 

The contact now sees the room with an orange door (identified with the owner's name) in the list of rooms, can open that room, if the role permits, invite others, and view, delete or add content.

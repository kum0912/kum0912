Room activity and Surveys 

You can save a log of those who entered and exited your room during an event as an attendance record from the Rooms tab in your MyRoom web account. You can also see your Survey results and download them for record keeping.

 

Log in to your account at the MyRoom home page. You can click on My Account or Login to bring up the Log In screen.     

Enter your email address (your account name) and click Next.

   
Enter your password and click Login.

   
Once logged in, you can click on the Rooms tab to see the Rooms details.

 

 
The list that appears shows all the rooms to which you have access. They are listed according to how you have them grouped in your MyRoom tray. Click the plus or minus sign next to the Group name to expand or contract each one.

 

 

Room activity  

 

Click on the Room activity link next to the room you are looking for. The Room activity listing opens. You can change the Start Date by clicking in the field and choosing the date in the box. Repeat for the End Date and then click on Search. A list of everyone who has entered and exited the room during the dates you specified is displayed, along with the time they entered and exited and how many minutes they were in the room. All hours listed are in UTC. If there is no exit time, it means that person is still in that room. Click on the title of a column to sort by that column, up and down. If the user has a MyRoom account, has it running and joins MyRoom from either the MyRoom tray or a link, then the Room Activity report includes the user's email address. You can click on Download results to download these results into an Excel spreadsheet which can then be manipulated as you want.

 

 

Surveys  

 

Click on the Surveys link next to the room the survey was held in. All surveys that have been run in the room are displayed along with the date the survey was last run and the number of times the survey has been run. Click on the name of the survey to see more details.

 

 
You can now see the results of each distinct survey that has been run. You can click on Download results to download these results into an Excel spreadsheet which can then be manipulated as you want.

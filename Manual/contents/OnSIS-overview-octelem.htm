Overview October elementary school

The October elementary school submission period applies to publicly funded elementary schools. This submission period captures elemental data based on an October 31 count date.  

 

Elementary students attending private schools do not get assigned OENs. Their October OnSIS submission period collects aggregate data based on the October 31 count date.  Please see Private schools for information.

 

If you are new to OnSIS, or as a refresher with each new OnSIS submission period, take a moment to review these important concepts.  

 

Things to check before commencing the 3 step ‘Create/Upload/Process’ cycle:

 

  • All students have OENs

  • Run pre-validation reports

  • Update educators in OnSIS

  • Get extracts from OnSIS

 

 

 

Upon completion of these steps:

 

  • Run verification reports

  • Sign off in OnSIS

Promotion history

 

Overview

The Promotion history option in a student’s record provides a quick summary of each previous school year. This includes information from all schools within the school board/division/district, as well as the student’s grade at the end of the year, their homeroom teacher, and whether they were promoted/retained/or transferred, along with attendance totals for that school year.

 

The promotion history is automatically recorded as part of the year-end roll over process (transfer to current). Since this information is not required, historical information (prior to using edsembli SIS) may be optionally added manually at any time.

 

This information is available for current, past and future students, and is student-specific, which means the information is the same regardless of the school that the student attends. Only the school that is the edsembli record owner can edit the student’s promotion history.

 

The Promotion history menu option is located in the Achievement submenu

 

 

Once selected, the Promotion history card, becomes available.

 

Promotion history

The Promotion history card will list the student’s promotion history in reverse chronological order. In most instances, this card is for information purposes and does not require any editing. 

 

 

Use the Add icon in the tool bar for a new entry. Each field is described below based on how the information is automatically populated during the year-end process of transfer to current.

 

  

 

Date completed: This date typically represents the last school day of the school year. If the student left the school mid-year, then the student’s retirement/exit date will be used.

 

Grade: This is the student’s current grade at the end of the school year, (or exit date), and represents the grade that the student was in during that particular school year.

 

Promotion type: This is a description as to whether the student successfully completed the school year. There are 3 entry choices from which to choose:

 

Promoted: the student was promoted to the next grade level (e.g. the student’s next year grade is higher than their current grade).

 

Retained: the student was not promoted to the next grade level (e.g. the student’s next year grade is the same or lower than their current grade, unless the student’s current grade is the highest grade in the school in which case it will default promoted).

 

Retired/transferred: the student left the school before the end of the school year.

 

Teacher: This is the student’s homeroom teacher at the end of the school year, if applicable. This is a text entry field so any teacher’s name may be manually entered.

 

Absent: This is the number of days the student was absent during the school year.

 

Excused: This is the number of days the student was excused during the school year.

 

Late: This is the number of times the student was late during the school year.

 

Possible: This is the number of possible schools days the student was expected to attend classes during the school year.

 

Once all the required fields are populated, the entry may be saved by clicking SAVE.

 

To edit an entry, click an entry to access the Edit option. When editing an entry, the delete icon will appear in the tool bar.

Overview

Often, the same fee is added to a group of students.  For example, all students in the school may be required to pay a student activity fee, or all students in grades 9 and higher have a locker fee, or all students in the visual arts class are going on a trip to the art gallery.  Fees can be added to a group of students quickly using the Group fee option.

 

When a group fee is created, you create a transaction name to describe the group fee, and provide the fee details such as the fee type and amount of the fee.  The group of students is selected from the standard student group options, including a random group which allows you to group students that aren’t typically grouped together.

 

Once the group fee is applied, each student in the group will have the fee automatically added to their fees.

 

Note:  Group fees does not check each student in the group to see if payment credits exist for the student, and therefore it will not ask if the payment credit can be applied as payment for the newly added fee.

 

The Group fees function will list all the group fees that have been created.  Select the group fee to access the Payment option. The students associated with that group fee will be listed.  It will default to display only the students who have not yet fully paid the fee.  Payment information may be entered and then applied to all or some of the students in the group.

 

To access Group fees choose Fees > Student fees and select OK on the Group fees card..

 

  

The Add fee page will appear:

 

  

The fields are the same as those required when adding a fee to a single student, except for one new field called Transaction name.  Enter a meaningful name that will help describe the group fee (e.g. Grade 8 trip to Ottawa). For details on the other fields, see Add a fee.

 

Below the fee fields, use the standard ‘Select by' area to select the group of students who require this fee.

 

The following options are available:  Alphabet, group, grade, homeroom, class, defined character, inquiry list , excursion list, advisor group, and bus routes.

 

Select SAVE at the bottom of the page to add the fee to all the students in the group.

 

Note:  Once you have added the group fee is added, you can make changes to individual students from the fees area of the student’s record, or by editing the group fee as explained below.

 

Edit a group fee

Row click the group fee and select Edit.

 

 

The Edit group fee form will appear:

 

 

 

Enter the adjusted fee information at the top of the Edit group fee form.

 

Select the students whose information will be adjusted or check the box in the header to place a check mark beside all students. When you select a student, the adjusted fee information will appear for that student. If necessary, you can edit the fee amount and comment for individual students after the box is checked.

  

Select SAVE at the bottom of the page to save the new fee information.

 

Important notes:

 

  • The  check box is not available for those students who have had fee amounts adjusted individually from the group fee (e.g. it was previously edited in the student’s record).  This is to prevent the accidental change to a student’s fees that were intentionally adjusted for another reason.  If the check box is not available, edits must be done via the fee’s area of the student’s records.
  • If a student has made a payment for this fee, and the fee amount is reduced such that the student paid more than the new fee, a payment credit is automatically created.
  • If taxes apply to this fee, the total with taxes will appear for each student.  It is possible to enter a  different fee amount for a student (by editing the fee amount beside the student’s name).  If this is necessary for a fee with taxes, enter the amount before taxes.  Taxes will be automatically calculated and the total including taxes will automatically appear for the fee.
  • The Group transaction card will show the fee amounts.  If students have different fee amounts because of edits after group fee was applied, it will how a range in this column (e.g. $10.00 - $15.00).

Voiding a group fee

The Void option can be used to delete the group fee.  It will void the fee for all students in the group.  Voided fees will not appear on the student’s record or any reports.  There is however an audit trail, and there is a record of this in the database for audit purposes if needed.

 

Row click a group fee and select Void. You'll need to answer a confirmation message.  

 

 

Note:  If the fee has a payment, when the fee is voided, the payment will be converted to a payment credit which can be refunded or applied to other fees.  See Payment credits for details.

 

Adding a payment for a group fee

Typically, payments are entered for each student individually, however, a group payment may be useful if someone collects the fee on behalf of the group of students (e.g. a teacher collects the field trip money) and then submits the money to the office for all students with that fee.

 

You can use the payment option apply the same payment amount to everyone who received the group fee by row clicking the group fee and selecting Payment.

 

 

Enter the payment information at the top of the Add group payment form.


Select the students whose information will be adjusted or check the box in the header to place a check mark beside all students. When you select a student, the adjusted fee information will appear for that student. If necessary, you can edit the fee amount and comment for individual students after the box is checked.

  

Select SAVE at the bottom of the page to save the new fee information.

Student reports > Enrolment > Homeroom summary

 

Homeroom summary

This report provides detailed grade and gender information for a school for a specific month. The report will provide totals for the school, but the information is broken down by homeroom. For the month selected, it will determine the number of students in the homeroom at the end of the month. For each homeroom, it will provide the number of males (M) and number of females (F) in the format #/# for each grade.

 

To create a Homeroom summary report, choose Student reports > Enrolment and select RUN on the Homeroom summary card.

 

 

The Student reports - Homeroom summary screen will appear.

 

 

Homeroom - Select an individual homeroom or choose a start and end with homeroom.

 

Include students registered - Select the registered type, both this year and next year. This year only (not returning), Next year only or Withdrawn (no longer enrolled this year).

 

Sort by - Schools may opt to print only specific homerooms (if run centrally, all homerooms are included), and specify the order of the homerooms (by room number, designation, or teacher).

   

Other - Specify from the following options.

 

Month: The month to be used in the report. Since this report is based on information at the end of the month, it will consider each student’s entry and exit dates to determine whether the student is enrolled at the end of the month. If your school tracks the dates when homerooms change, homeroom changes are recorded in the internal transfers area (in the Enrolment area of the student’s record). Grade changes are also tracked in the internal transfers area. Grade and homeroom internal transfers, if they exist, will be used to determine each student’s homeroom and grade at the end of the month. Students without a homeroom at the end of the month will appear in a homeroom called “Unassigned”.

 

Include: (Does not appear for elementary schools) All students will be included by default. Secondary schools  will have the option to generate separate reports for elementary or secondary students by changing this field to Elementary students or Secondary students. The student’s entry date to secondary school will be used to determine whether the student is considered elementary or secondary on the specified date. If this report is run centrally, the report may include either all students, elementary students, or secondary students.

Student records > Enrolment > Internal transfers


Overview

As certain fields are changed for a student, the date of the change is recorded in the internal transfers along with the original and new values assigned to the student. The information tracked through internal transfers is used when running reports on dates in the past where it is important to report the value the student had on a prior date. Internal transfers are provided for information purposes only and typically do not require any editing. Some transfers should be edited if the date of the transfer is incorrect (e.g. the school did not change the student data on the date it happened, and the date of the transfer needs to be corrected so that it is accurate for a report).

 

Choose Enrolment > Internal transfers from the student records submenu.

 

 

 

Internal transfers are date specific entries that record when a student had a change to a key fields that may be used in date specific reporting. For example, when a student's grade advances from grade 7 to 8 over the summer, there would be an internal transfer dated August 15 for grade 7 to grade 8. If a report were run to determine a student's grade the previous June, the student would be considered in grade 7, however, if the report were run for September, the student would be considered grade 8.

 

When a card includes fields that are tracked, you will see the Internal transfer icon on the tool bar.

 

The Internal transfers submenu option provides all tracked information on one convenient card.

 

Internal transfers

The Internal transfer card uses a tab strip to organize internal transfers based on the type of information that is tracked. Internal transfers are automatically recorded for the following types of information listed below. The internal transfer is created when the field (piece of information) is changed for the student.

 

 

Each transfer includes the date of the change, the student’s value prior to the change (original value) and the student’s value after the change (new value). Dates are based on the school’s calendar date, not the system date. Internal transfers are listed in chronological order. Typically, internal transfers are not added, but sometimes it may be necessary to edit the date of the internal transfer (e.g. if the change was not made on the correct date).

 

Select Schools

If the student’s record exists in other schools, use the drop down arrow to select a different school; you'll then see the other school’s internal transfer information.

 

Internal transfer types

Use the tabs at the top of the Internal transfers main screen to navigate between the internal transfer types. Internal transfer types vary by school and province.

 

Edit internal transfer

Row click the internal transfer and select Edit from the menu

 

 

You will see the appropriate fields for the selected internal transfer.

 

Click Save to store changes or Cancel to return to the main editing screen.

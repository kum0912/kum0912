Overview continuing education for day schools (IILE/Literacy & numeracy)

The Continuing education for day school (IILE/Literacy & numeracy) submission period applies to publicly funded day schools offering these programs and tracking students’ enrolment in these continuing education programs:

 

  • International & Indigenous Languages Elementary (IILE) Program
  • Literacy & numeracy programs

 

Days schools need to have their school configuration set up to enable access to these continuing education registers and this OnSIS submission period.

 

This submission period captures elemental data based on an August 31 count date.  

 

If you are new to OnSIS, or as a refresher with each new OnSIS submission period, take a moment to review these important concepts.  

 

Things to check before commencing the 3 step ‘Create/Upload/Process’ cycle:

 

  • All students have OENs (except parents enrolled in literacy/numeracy to support their child)
  • Run pre-validation reports

 

 

Upon completion of these steps:

 

  • Run verification reports
  • Sign off in OnSIS

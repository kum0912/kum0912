Scheduling next year > Course selections > Individual student

All functionality related to a specific student’s course selections is available from the course selections option in the student’s record.  For quick access to this course selection information, an Individual student card is provided from the Scheduling next year area.

 

To access individual student course selections, choose Scheduling next year, Course selections. Select Go on the Individual student card.

 

 

Enter the student’s name using the standard select student form found in student records and the student’s record will open focussed on the Course selections option.  See Student records - Course selections for more information.

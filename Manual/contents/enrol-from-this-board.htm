Enrol students from this board/division

 

To see a list of students waiting to be enrolled into your board/division, choose Student tools, Add new student and

select GO on the Enrol students from this board/division card.

 

 

The Enrol students from this board/division screen will appear and the Students to be transferred card will show students waiting to be transferred. Select the checkbox beside the student(s) you want to enrol and then select the ENROL button.

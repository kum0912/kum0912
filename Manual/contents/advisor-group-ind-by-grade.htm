 Scheduling next year > Advisor groups > Individual by grade

Advisor groups are similar to homerooms where a room and teacher(s) is/are assigned to the advisor group. The name of the group can be anything the school wants. Some schools use advisor groups to indicate the guidance counsellor assigned to the student. Some schools have students meet regularly in groups where the students meet with an advisory teacher to discuss general school related topics.

 

This option is just like assigning homerooms using the Individual by grade option, except that it is assigning advisor groups.

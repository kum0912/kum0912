Update educators in OnSIS

The Update educators in OnSIS card has no functionality other than to serve as a reminder to update the educators in OnSIS before beginning to use the SIS to create batch files. This card is available for these submission periods:

  • October elementary school

  • October secondary school

  • March elementary school

  • March secondary school

  • June elementary school

  • June secondary school

 

For these submission periods, selecting the INFO button on this card will open this page of the manual.

 

Before generating any extracts in the OnSIS application, and before submitting any batch files to OnSIS for processing, be sure to update the educators in OnSIS.  Educator info in OnSIS should represent the teacher’s info as of the count date corresponding to the OnSIS submission period (e.g. October 31, March 31, June 30, August 31 for continuing ed/summer school).

 

You can perform the Updating educators process directly in the OnSIS application, or through the school/board’s HR application. Any new educators will need their newly assigned Ministry Education Numbers (MEN #s) entered into their staff record in the SIS.  

 

If you are unsure how to update educators in OnSIS, refer to your board for assistance, or contact the OnSIS help desk.  

 

Private schools will typically update this information by manually entering the info using the OnSIS application. These are very general instructions:  

  1. Log into the Ministry’s OnSIS application.
  2. Within OnSIS, select Data Collection, Add/Update School Submission (from the top menu bar).
  3. Select the Submission Period.
  4. Select Educator.
  5. From the list of educators already in OnSIS for the school, select the educator that needs updating or use the Add Educator button to add a new teacher.  
  6. If you need to withdraw a teacher who is no longer at the school as of the OnSIS count date, update the educator and set their Teaching/Non-Teaching field to “Not Applicable”, set the Withdrawal Type to the applicable reason and enter their last day at the school in the Assignment End Date.

 

Once the list of Educators is up to date and reflects who is teaching at the school for this submission period, you are ready to get extracts from OnSIS.

 

Before creating any OnSIS batch files, make sure that all teachers have their Ministry assigned MEN #s recorded in their staff records in the edsembli SIS. The MEN# is recorded in Staff records > Personal > Name.

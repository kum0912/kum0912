Available rooms

 

 

When building the school’s master timetable, sometimes classes are scheduled without teachers and/or rooms. Later, when the room is assigned to the class, it is important that the room is available (e.g. room is not already occupied with another class).

 

From the Available rooms option, the user selects a specific time in the school’s schedule. This is done by selecting the semester, term, day, period you want to search and then selecting the search button. If applicable, you may select multiple semesters, terms, days or periods. For example, if searching semester 1, terms 1 & 2, period 1, days 1 &2, the room must be available in semester 1, period 1 in both terms and both days.

 

Choose Scheduling next year > Schedule classes and select Go on the Available rooms card.

 

 

You'll see the Scheduling next year - available rooms page.  Specify the semester, term, day and period then select the search icon.

 

 

 

The available rooms will be listed along with the classes occupying the rooms in each period of that semester/term/day.  Multiple cards will appear for each semester/term/day searched.

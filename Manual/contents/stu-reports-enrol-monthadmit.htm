Student reports > Enrolment > Monthly admissions

 

Monthly admissions

This report provides a list of students who are new to the school during any given month of the current school year. The student’s entry date to this school is used to determine when a student is newly admitted to a school.

 

To create a Monthly admissions report, choose Student reports > Enrolment and select RUN on the Monthly admissions card.

 

 

The Student reports - Monthly admissions screen will appear.

 

  

Other - You'll see these fields:

 

Month: The month to be used in the report. Since this report is based on information at the end of the month, it will consider each student’s entry and exit dates to determine whether the student was admitted to the school during the selected month.

  

Include: (Does not appear for elementary schools) All students will be included by default. Secondary schools  will have the option to generate separate reports for elementary or secondary students by changing this field to Elementary students or Secondary students. The student’s entry date to secondary school will be used to determine whether the student is considered elementary or secondary on the specified date. If this report is run centrally, the report may include either all students, elementary students, or secondary students.

 

The resulting report will include student information from the student’s record. The student’s age is the age of the student on their admission date.

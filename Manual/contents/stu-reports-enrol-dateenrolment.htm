Enrolment on a date

This report provides a list of students who are enrolled in the school on a specific date.  The student’s entry and retirement/exit dates to this school are used to determine whether a student is enrolled on the specific date.

 

If a date from a previous school year is specified, it will consider students with the Maplewood status set to Past as well.  The names of the past students will be reported first, followed by the students who are  in the current records.  The total number of past student, total number of current students, and the sum of both will appear on the report.

 

To create a Monthly admissions report, choose Student reports > Enrolment and select RUN on the Monthly admissions card.

 

 

 

The Student reports - Enrolment on a date screen will appear.

 

  

Other - You'll see these fields on the Other card:

 

Date:  The date to be used in the report. It will consider each student’s entry and exit dates to determine whether the student was enrolled in the school on the specified date. Please note that dates beyond the end of the school calendar may not be selected, however any date from the past is allowed.

 

Include: (Does not appear for elementary schools) All students will be included by default. Secondary schools  will have the option to generate separate reports for elementary or secondary students by changing this field to Elementary students or Secondary students. The student’s entry date to secondary school will be used to determine whether the student is considered elementary or secondary on the specified date. If this report is run centrally, the report may include either all students, elementary students, or secondary students.

 

The resulting report will include each student’s name, provincial student number and Maplewood status. Ontario schools will also include a column for the student’s Board Resident Status.

Principal’s monthly report  

This report provides detailed enrolment and attendance information for a school for a specific month. The report will provide totals for the school, but the information is broken down by grade.

 

To create a Principal’s monthly report, choose Student reports > Enrolment and select RUN on the Principal’s monthly report card.

 

 

The Student reports - Age summary screen will appear.

 

  

Other - You'll see these fields on the Other card:

 

Month:  The month to be used in the report.  Since this report is based on information at the end of the month, it will consider each student’s entry and exit dates to determine whether the student is enrolled at the end of the month.  Grade internal transfers, if they exist, will be used to determine each student’s grade at the end of the month.

 

Include: (Does not appear for elementary schools)  All students will be included by default. Secondary schools  will have the option to generate separate reports for elementary or secondary students by changing this field to Elementary students or Secondary students.  The student’s entry date to secondary school will be used to determine whether the student is considered elementary or secondary on the specified date.  If this report is run centrally, the report may include either all students, elementary students, or secondary students.

For the month selected, it will provide the following information for each grade:

 

Enrolment:  This is the number of students in that grade that are enrolled in the school at the end of the month.

 

Grade changes are dated and recorded in the internal transfers area (in the Enrolment area of the student’s record).  If grade internal transfers exist, they will be used to determine each student’s grade at the end of the month.

 

The student’s entry and retirement/exit dates to this school are used to determine whether a student is enrolled at the end of the month.

 

Actual days attendance:  Each student’s attendance is counted and summed to determine the actual days attendance.  For example, if student 1 attends 15.5 days during the month, and student 2 attends 16 days, if there were only 2 students in that grade, the total would be 31.5 days.

 

Students with period attendance will be calculated based on the am/pm equivalent attendance.  For example, if a student was absent period 1 and 2 of a 4 period day, that day’s attendance would be 0.5.

 

Possible days attendance:  Each day where a student is expected to be at school is counted for the month.  Each student’s possible days is summed to determine the possible days attendance.  For example, if student 1 has 20 possible days, and student has 20 possible days, if there were only 2 students in that grade, the total would be 40 days.

 

If a student’s entry date is after the start of the month, the student would have a lower number of possible days.

 

Students not enrolled at the end of the month are excluded from the report.

 

The report gives a % attended value for elementary students, junior & senior high school students, and an overall % attended value for the school.

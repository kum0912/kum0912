Overview summer school

The Summer school submission period applies to publicly funded schools offering summer school programs for credit courses delivered through classroom or cooperative education, credit recovery, additional preparation for changing course type, math courses for changing course types, and non-credit summer school programs for pupils with developmental disabilities. Students registered in these summer programs are reported in this continuing education register:

 

  • Summer School Register

 

Schools need to have their school configuration set up with a school operating type of ‘summer school’ to enable access to these continuing education registers and this OnSIS submission period.

 

This submission period captures elemental data based on an August 31 count date.  

 

If you are new to OnSIS, or as a refresher with each new OnSIS submission period, take a moment to review these important concepts.  

 

Things to check before commencing the 3 step ‘Create/Upload/Process’ cycle:

 

  • All students have OENs  
  • Run pre-validation reports

 

 

Upon completion of these steps:

 

  • Run verification reports
  • Sign off in OnSIS

Attendance - Overview

 

Every school expects all students to attend school regularly and attend all of their classes when at school. Students are considered present (attending school) unless attendance information is entered indicating otherwise. Attendance may be entered by the school office or by the teachers in the classrooms.

 

Before using attendance, please take a moment to read these sections:

 

Important concepts

Common attendance features

 

Each school will adapt its own attendance procedures and policies regarding unexplained absences, however many schools follow similar daily processes. This is our suggested daily processes for the school office and the teachers:

 

  1. Phone calls/notes regarding student attendance are entered by the school office on an individual basis (see Enter individual student attendance).
  2. School office enters attendance for groups of students with known attendance such as field trips, sport events, bus delays (see Enter Group/excursion attendance).
  3. Teachers take attendance as soon as class begins (see Enter homeroom attendance or Enter class attendance). Even if no students are absent, the teacher must indicate that they have taken attendance.
  4. Office checks to see whether all teachers have taken attendance.  
  5. Office examines unexplained absences and contacts parents if appropriate.
  6. Steps 3 to 5 are repeated for each time attendance is taken.

 

Please see Typical daily attendance process for more information.

 

To access the attendance functionality, choose Attendance. The Attendance section has these cards organized into 4 main areas.

 

Enter attendance

These are the applications that are used in the daily attendance entry.

 

  

Call Home

This is where you can see the absent students and, if required, call home to find out the reason why the student is not at school.

 

 

Reports

These are the reports specifically for attendance information. These reports are also found in the Student reports area from the main menu. Each attendance report is presented in a card with a brief description of the report.

   

Setup

These are the setup applications that are usually set up once and then are occasionally used as needed. These applications are also found in the Setup area from the main menu.

Overview night/continuing education school

 

The Night/continuing education school submission period applies to publicly funded day schools offering these continuing education programs:

 

  • Continuing Education Credit Courses
  • Correspondence/Self-Study/e-Learning Courses
  • International & Indigenous Languages Elementary (IILE) Program
  • Literacy & numeracy programs
  • Adult Indigenous Language Instruction

 

Schools need to have their school configuration set up with a school operating type of ‘continuing education’ to enable access to these continuing education registers and this OnSIS submission period.

 

This submission period captures elemental data based on an August 31 count date.  

 

If you are new to OnSIS, or as a refresher with each new OnSIS submission period, take a moment to review these important concepts.  

 

Things to check before commencing the 3 step ‘Create/Upload/Process’ cycle:

 

  • All students have OENs  
  • Run pre-validation reports

 

 

Upon completion of these steps:

 

  • Run verification reports
  • Sign off in OnSIS

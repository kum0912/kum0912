Scheduling next year > Schedule classes > Available teachers

When building the school’s master timetable, sometimes classes are scheduled without teachers and/or rooms.  Later, when the teacher is assigned to the class, it is important that the teacher is available (e.g. teacher is not already busy teaching another class).

 

From the Available teachers option, the user selects a specific time in the school’s schedule.  This is done by selecting the semester, term, day, period you want to search and then selecting the search button. If applicable, you may select multiple semesters, terms, days or periods. For example, if searching semester 1, terms 1 & 2, period 1, days 1 &2, the teacher must be available in semester 1, period 1 in both terms and both days.

 

Choose Scheduling next year > Schedule classes and select Go on the Available teachers card.

 

 

You'll see the Scheduling next year - available teachers page.

 

 

The available teachers will be listed along with the classes the teacher is scheduled to teach in each period of that semester/term/day.  Multiple cards will appear for each semester/term/day searched.

 

 

Note:  This is an informational list of available teachers, and only includes those staff members who are flagged as teachers. To assign a teacher to a class, edit the class in the setup table.

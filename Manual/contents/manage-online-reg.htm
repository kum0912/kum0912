Manage online registrations

SIS users can register students in their school when a registration has been received from the web service. User will select the Manage online registrations option under the Student tools menu, and will be presented with a list of students to be registered. Once a student is selected from this list, the search function for Add new students will be performed, and if a match is found they will be presented. If no match is found, the user will be prompted to Enrol this student as a new student.

 

There are three other buttons in this interface, including View submission, which allows the user to view the contents of the actual JSON file that was sent from the Web service, View Reg form, which allows the user to view the values the user entered in the Online registration form (NOT produced in Maplewood, produced via a third party) or Send to school, which allows the user to select another school in the board to send the student to (for example, if the parent has registered the student in the wrong school for their catchment area).

Enrolled Students Concurrent Online Registration

If the already entered students in the school are provided access to Maplewood connectEd Students, with appropriate rights granted, those students can register at another school in your board.  This new option will allow the student to see all their concurrent school registrations in the board (present, and future).  The Registration option will also provide new functionality to the student; the ability to request/submit a registration at another school within the board.

  

Once the student has provided this Request for Registration at another school, the other school can accept or decline the registration (through the Enrol Students from this Board tool).  Once enrolled in a new school, the student can then (and only then) proceed with entering their courses selections for that new school.

Overview of business process:

Dependencies:

• Student must be enrolled in one school within the board (at least).

• For students with concurrent enrolments within a board (more than one), the Registration option is ONLY available at the Home school.

1. School provides students access to Maplewood connectEd’s Registration option

School will have a new option under School Information - Configuration to Allow Online Registrations.  In Maplewood connectEd’s security, you must set new permission for the Students to get access to the new Registration screen at the student’s home school.  We would suggest setting these in your templates for Student so that any new student will get this by default.

2. Student enters their request for registration

The student logs onto Maplewood connectEd.  

The student will have a Registration menu option, (as shown below). It will show the student’s request(s) for registrations at another school(s) in the board (which will typically be empty to start), followed by the school(s) where the student is currently enrolled in the board and any choices (course selections) already entered at enrolled schools.  Will not display choices for the school that student is currently logged in to.

As shown below, from the Request Registration at another School area, the student will use the drop down lists to select the school they would like to attend (e.g. Maplewood Summer School) and for which school year.  Dates corresponding to either this school year or next school year are available for selection).

 

Notes:

If the student has a school listed in the Request area, and it is locked, this means that the school office has indicated that the student is leaving this school to attend that other school (e.g. the school is recorded in the destination school but the receiving school has not yet enrolled the student by running the process to enroll students from this board.  

3. The Requested School(s) Accepts the Student’s Registration Request

Using the utility Enrol Students from This Board, the requested school can accept (or decline) the student’s request for enrolment.   

 

Note: The List of Students to be Transferred will depend upon the selected Maplewood Status.  If This Year and Next or This Year Only is selected, the list of students to be transferred will include the students who have requested an enrollment at this school for the current school year (ie. 2016/17).  If Next Year Only is selected, the list of students to be transferred will include the students who have requested an enrollment at this school for the upcoming school year (ie. 2017/18).  

In both instances, students with a Destination School of the current and/or an existing entry in the office Pre-registration will also appear on the list of Students to be Transferred.

Once enrolled, the Requested School will need to grant permission for the student to enter Choices (course selections).  If the school has set up default permission, no action is required as these are inherited with the enrolment entry.

4. Student enters their course selections for the newly enrolled school

From the student’s Home School Maplewood connectEd login (the student will be able to enter their course selections (choices) for the newly enrolled school(s) within the Registration menu option. Once the school has accepted the student’s registration request the school will appear in the Schools area of the Registration option (shown below).

In the Choices column, click the Edit button for the school you wish to view or edit choices. The school’s name will appear in the title ‘Student Choices for XXX School’.   The student will use the Add and Remove buttons to enter course selections (choices).

 

Notes:

Courses available for selection are from the courses in that school’s Next Year’s courses table.  It will include only those courses that have the boxed checked off in the “Show in Online Selection” field.  

If the student is enrolled for the next school year, the entry of courses can only begin after the school has initialized scheduling in preparation for the next school year.

5. New school creates timetable for the student

Once the course selections (Choices) are entered, the school will use that information to create a timetable for the student.

For students who are registered for the current school year, the school will need to specifically edit the student’s timetable in order for the students to be enrolled in classes.  Edit timetable has a feature where the student’s unscheduled choices can be displayed so that the student’s class sections can be easily picked, or the timetable suggestions feature can be used to automatically build the student’s timetable.

For students who are registered for the next school year, the timetables are created as part of the school’s scheduling process, just like all other students who are attending the school next year.

Scheduling next year > Resolve student conflicts > Individual student

 

 

Resolving student conflicts is done late in the scheduling process, specifically AFTER the school has completed these 2 processes for the very last time:

 

  • the classes are scheduled (the master timetable is complete) AND
  • the load students process has been run for the last time.

 

Use the Individual student option to quickly go into a student’s next year timetable.

 

Choose Scheduling next year > Resolve student conflicts.

 

 

Select Go on the Individual student card

 

Upon selecting the desired student, it will open the student’s record in edit next year’s timetable, with the ‘showing preferred requests not yet placed in timetable’ view.

Marks & comments - Previous years

Overview

This Marks & Comments option in a student’s record provides access to a student's marks & comments for previous school years if report card and other mark info was entered using edsembli SIS. This achievement info is automatically recorded as part of the year-end roll over process (Archive attendance & achievement). Mark & comment information is for viewing purposes only. It may not be edited.

 

Only the school years where the student has archived achievement entries will be available for the student. If the student is enrolled in multiple schools in a school year, the school drop down field will appear where you can select to see the marks and comments from a previous year at another school.

 

This Marks & comments option is located in the Previous years submenu.

 

 

Once selected, the Marks & comments - Previous years card, becomes available.

 

Marks & comments - Previous years

This option is very similar to the current year's marks & comments, however it is providing a view only presentation of the achievement info from a previous school year. It will provide a school year field where you can select from the school years where achievement information exists for the student.

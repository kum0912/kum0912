Overview

The schools menu option is available for all students (past, present and future students). Choose Enrolment > Schools from the submenu.

 

 

You will see one or both of these 2 cards. Schools, and Requests for concurrent enrolment.

 

Schools

The Schools card shows a record of the student’s schooling at this board/division/district. All students will have this card, and the contents of the card will be the same for all schools.

 

The Schools card contains a chronological list of all the schools in which this student has been enrolled at in your board/division/district. Each school on this list will have access to this student’s record. If a student returns to a school after a period of non-enrolment at that school, a new entry (row) will appear for each time the student returns to the school .

 

The information is not editable because it is automatically obtained from the contents of the student’s entry/exit info at each school. If edits are needed, access the student’s record from the school, then choose Enrolment > Student enrolment submenu option.

 

 

 

See Enrolment > Student enrolment for additional info on the contents of this Schools card.

Requests for concurrent enrolment

The Requests for concurrent enrolment card is only available to those students where this school is the edsembli record owner. Students who attended the school in previous years (past students) do not have this card either. You can use this card when a student wants to register at another school (e.g. typically summer school) but the edsembli record owner will remain the home school. It is simply a location to request enrolment at another school in the board/division, so that the other school can see the request and accept the student’s enrolment.

 

 

  1. To add a concurrent request, select the Add icon in the tool bar. Enter the name of the school where the student wants to enroll. Those schools in your board/division (other than the home school) matching the entered text will appear.

 

`

 
  1. Select the school.
 

 
  1. Select SAVE and the Request for concurrent enrolment card will appear.
 

 

The school can now enrol this student at their school using Student tools > Add student > Enrol students from this board. When the student is enrolled at the other school, the request will be removed from this card.

 

If the student no longer wants to request this registration at the other school, you may remove the request by selecting the row with the request and then selecting Discard:

 

 

Note: If the student were leaving this school to attend another school, this is not where the request to register the student at another school is made. In those situations, the student is leaving this school and therefore the destination school would be available to indicate where the student is going to. These requests appear on this card as well, however, they will be locked as they cannot be edited (removed) from here.

Student reports > Popular > Siblings

 

Popular Reports

The student reports in the popular section include those reports that schools most often generate beyond the traditional class and timetable reports.

 

To print click the Printer icon that you will see in the bottom right corner.

 

Siblings

To create a Siblings report choose Student reports > Popular and select RUN on the Siblings card.

 

 

The Student reports - Siblings screen will appear with a tabbed menu for report option selection. Select an option, Select All students, Youngest student in household, or Oldest student in household and the list will automatically display in the Siblings grid.

OSR request

 

Overview

 

Most schools have an official record for each student enrolled in their school. This is typically a file folder containing paper copies of pertinent data for the student. The official record will have a province-specific name (e.g. Ontario Student Record (OSR), Cumulative Folder, Personal Student Record).

 

Schools can track requests for a student’s official record from another school, as well as track requests to send the official record to another school (e.g. if the student is leaving your school). As well, the official record identifies which school is the record holder. Information is available for current, past and future students.

 

Official record information is school-specific, and may be changed to reflect the student’s activity at this school. Any school-specific data changes will not impact the student’s information at another school.

 

Choose Enrolment > OSR request from the submenu.

 

 

 

OSR request

The OSR request card contains an indicator as to whether or not the student’s Official Student Record (OSR) is located at this school.  It also contains date fields that the school may use to track the movement of the OSR to and from other schools, and the name of the school where the OSR was sent (if applicable).

 

The following fields are available when editing the OSR request card.  This information is optional and is recorded for information purposes only (except in Saskatchewan where the first field (OSR at this school) is reported as part of the provincial data collection).

 

Official Student Record (OSR) at School Name: This switch  is set to Yes if the student’s OSR is located at this school.  This school’s name will appear in the field name.

 

    • Requested by this school: The date you requested to have the OSR sent to this school.  
    • Received by this school: The date you received the student’s OSR at this school.  
    • Requested by destination school: The date another school request the student’s OSR to be sent to that other school.  
    • Sent to destination school: The date the student’s OSR was sent to the other school.  
    • Destination school: The name of the school where the OSR was sent.

Independent study & e-learning ADE

 

 

The Independent study & e-learning ADE option provides a report that mirrors the OnSIS independent study (IS) enrolment detail and summary report. It shows Average Daily Enrolment (ADE) info for IS students and home/private schooled students taking e-learning courses at regular day schools.

 

To ensure that IS & e-learning ADE data is being captured correctly for OnSIS reporting, Ontario publicly funded schools and boards may create this OnSIS validation report - without having to access the OnSIS application, and without the need for OnSIS extract files. The report also allows the user to change submissions periods (report dates) to see the impact on the OnSIS reporting.

 

The ADE calculations on this report will be based on the information recorded on the Independent Study and e-Learning Register for Day School Pupils. Refer to the instructions on this register for details on ADE for these students.

 

To access this report, select Ontario Reporting > Independent study > Independent study & e-learning ADE.

 

 

Select Go on the Independent study & e-learning ADE card to see this form:

 

 

Select the OnSIS submission period (October, March or June). The date will automatically change to match the current year’s count dates (e.g. October 31, March 31, June 30).

 

Select the Print button located at the bottom of the form. Once the report has been submitted to the task queue, you may leave this application or generate other reports. You will receive notification when your report is ready. Reports are saved in file manager under Provincial > Independent Study.

 

 

Hint: See previously created reports by selecting the folder icon to open file manager at this location.

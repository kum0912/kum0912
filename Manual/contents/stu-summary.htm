Overview

When you select a student the summary page will appear, giving a quick overview of the most commonly accessed information for a student.

 

Each grouping of information is presented on a separate card. The DETAILS button on each card provides a quick link to more detailed information by taking you to that specific area in the student's record.

 

The cards on the Summary page may vary depending on whether the student has information to display and if the content is appropriate for the province.

 

Emergency note

If the student has an emergency note and/or medical alerts entered, they will appear on a card at the top of the student's summary page.

 


Use the DETAILS button to go directly to the student’s Alerts & notes page in the Emergency area.

 

 

Custody

If the student is flagged as having a special custody situation, the student’s custody note will appear on this card.

 


Use the DETAILS button to go directly to the student’s custody page in the Family & Contacts area.

 

Current location

If the student’s timetable contains at least one class in a period today, the Current location card is available to indicate where the student may be located right now by showing the class, room and teacher information for the student’s class during the current period (based on the current time compared to the school’s period times). The student's attendance in that class is also displayed as a single letter corresponding to the attendance code (e.g. P for present) for students with 'by period' attendance, and 2 attendance codes will appear for students with 'morning/afternoon' attendance. If the student is not assigned a class in the current period, the words “Student is not in any class at this time” will appear. The drop down arrow beside the period may be used to change to a different period. Use the CLASSES button to go directly to the student’s Class info > Timetable option (showing today's timetable). Use the TODAY'S ATTENDANCE to go directly to the student's Attendance > Attendance option.

 


Contacts

The student’s contacts are grouped into the following tabs that appear on the Contact card if appropriate for the student. A contact will appear in the tab under the following conditions:

 

PARENT(S)/GUARDIAN(S): Contact is flagged as legal guardian.

CUSTODIAN(S): Contact is flagged as custodian.

EMERGENCY: Contact is flagged as emergency contact.

NON-CUSTODIAL PARENT(S): Contact is flagged as non-custodial parent.

LIVES WITH STUDENT: Contact’s address is the same as the student.

SCHOOL CLOSURE: Contact is flagged as school closure contact.

DOCTOR: Contact has the Doctor contact type.

 

When multiple contacts appear on a tab, the contact order is used to determine the order, however, emergency and school closure contacts will consider the emergency order and closure order instead.



Use the DETAILS button to go directly to the student’s Contacts page.

 

You may print the student's contacts. Select the More icon and choose the Print option.

 

 

Address

The student’s address info is shown in the Address card. If a student has distinct addresses for mailing or physical or permanent address, each will appear in the card.

 


Permanent addresses apply to those students who live elsewhere outside of the school year. Different mailing and physical addresses sometimes occur for students with rural addresses where the mail is delivered in a different town (city) than the town used to physically locate the student’s house, making one address for both purposes impossible.

 

Click the address to locate the address on a map.

 

Use the DETAILS button to go directly to the student’s Contact info page.

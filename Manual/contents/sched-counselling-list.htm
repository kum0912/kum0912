Scheduling next year > Course selections > Counselling list

This report lists next year’s courses along with the number of students who have selected each course as a preferred course and the number who have selected it as an alternate course.  Courses that have no student requests are excluded from the report.

 

Each course may be expanded to identify the students who have requested the course.  Selecting a student will open a new browser window with that student’s record opened to the course selections option.

 

This report is helpful when school guidance staff need to counsel students, for example when a course is no longer being offered due to insufficient interest.  Quick access to the student’s record also provides access to all of the student’s information that guidance staff may want to reference, including completed courses, current classes, mark information, and graduation requirements.   

 

To access the counselling list, choose Scheduling next year, Course selections. Select Go on the Counselling list card.

 

 

The counselling list will appear similar to this example below:  

 

 

Select the Show more icon (to the left of the course code) to see information about the students who have requested the course as one of their preferred or alternate course selections.

 

 

 

Each student will appear with their name, selection type (whether the course is a preferred or alternate course selection), this year grade and next year grade, as well the list of the student’s preferred courses and alternate courses.

 

Select a student from the list to open that student’s record in a new browser window.  It will be opened to the course selections option for easy access to edit the student’s course selections, however you may browse to other options as needed.  See For more information see Course selections.  

 

When the course is expanded to show the list of students, an app bar will appear for that course.  Select the Export icon from the app bar to export the counselling list for that course, in csv format.  

 

 

Hint:  See List course selections if you want to export the same student information for all students in the school, rather than just the students in one specific course.

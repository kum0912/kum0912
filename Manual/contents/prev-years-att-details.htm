 Overview

The Attendance details option in a student’s record provides access to a student's daily attendance information for previous school years if attendance was entered using edsembli SIS. This attendance info is automatically recorded as part of the year-end roll over process (Archive attendance & achievement). Attendance information is for viewing purposes only. It may not be edited.

Only the school years where the student has archived attendance entries will be available for the student. If the student is enrolled in multiple schools in a school year, the school drop down field will appear where you can select to see the attendance from a previous year at another school.

The Attendance details option is located in the Previous years sub-menu.

Once selected, the Attendance details card, becomes available.

 

 Attendance details

The Attendance details card will provide a school year field where you can select from the school years where attendance entries exist for the student. Since attendance may be recorded for the student using the 'by period' or 'morning/afternoon' method, the presentation of the information and the options will vary based on the attendance method for that school year.

 

'By period' attendance

Here is an example of a student whose attendance was recorded using the 'by period' attendance method. It will default to show the Period attendance presentation as shown below.

Dates will appear in rows for any date where the student has attendance other than present (P). The student's attendance in each period is shown. Homeroom attendance, if taken, will appear as well.  

 

The period attendance may be presented in morning/afternoon format (as shown later) by selecting the Morning/afternoon translation option. The school's attendance parameters will define the morning/afternoon periods and then the period attendance will be converted into morning/afternoon attendance. For example, if a student is absent for only the first period of the day, the student will be translated into Late for the morning and Present for the afternoon.

 

Select the Exam day attendance option to see the student's morning/afternoon attendance during those dates in that year's calendar that were defined as exam days. Only dates that have attendance other than present will appear.

Scheduling next year > Course selections > Course tally

This report provides a tally of the number of students who have selected each course as one of their preferred courses.  For example, if a course ADA101 has 41 students who have selected the course as one of their preferred courses, and 15 students who have selected it as one of their alternate courses, the tally will show 41 students.

 

The course tally will help the school identify courses that may have insufficient interest from the students that the course will not be offered.  It may also be used to help determine the number of class sections required for each course.  The report may be sorted by any column and may be exported.

  

To access the course tally, choose Scheduling next year, Course selections. Select Go on the Course tally card.

 

 

The course tally will appear similar to this example below:  

 

Select any of the column headers to change the sort order.  For example, sort by the ‘Count’ column to see the courses in ascending or descending order based on the number of students selecting that course.

 

Select the Export icon from the app bar to export in csv format.

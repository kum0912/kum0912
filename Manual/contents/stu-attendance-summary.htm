Student records > Attendance > Summary

Overview

The Attendance option in a student’s record contains information related to the student’s attendance. You can enter and modify attendance from the student’s record, but typically this is not where you enter daily attendance.  If you are not already familiar with the attendance functionality, see the Attendance section of the manual.

 

The Summary option contains only information for the current school year, and therefore the option is not applicable to past students who attended the school in previous years.

 

Choose Attendance > Summary from the submenu of the student’s record.

 

 

The student’s attendance information is summarized on this page.

Students attending multiple schools this year

If a student attends more than 1 school in your board/division/district in the current school year, distinct attendance information and attendance notes will be recorded at each school. You can select a different school with attendance records from the School drop down list.

 

 

Summary

The Summary card presents a summary of a student’s attendance considering all attendance up to and including the current date as explained in Common attendance features - Summary.  Future attendance, if entered, is excluded. You can view by Year, Term and Semester. References to 'Excused' typically represent attendance where the student is present to school but is excused from going to the class due to other school related activities (e.g. sports, field trips).

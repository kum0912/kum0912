Scheduling next year > Course selections > Course analysis by grade

This report lists next year’s courses along with the number of students in each grade who have selected the course as one of their preferred or alternate course selections.  The grades in the report are based on the students’ next years grades.  Courses that have no student requests are excluded from the report.

 

This report will help the school identify students who have selected courses outside their grade level. For example, a grade 12 course showing a number in the grade 9 column might be an indication of a student selecting the wrong course.   

  

To access the course analysis by grade list, choose Scheduling next year > Course selections.

 

 

 

Depending upon which course selection types are to be analyzed in the report, select either the PREFERRED or ALTERNATE button.

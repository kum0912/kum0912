Overview

The Custody option in a student’s record contains student-specific information, i.e. information that is the same regardless of what school the student attends. Only the school that is the record owner can edit this information.

 

Choose Family & contacts > Custody from the submenu.

 

 

The student’s custody information is grouped and presented on separate cards.  There is a card titled Custody along with contact cards for each of the student’s contacts that are flagged as either a legal guardian or a custodian as described below.

 

Custody

The Custody card contains a note that the school may used to record notes regarding the student’s custodial situation.  Only those students with the special custody flag set to Yes may have a note entered.

 

 

By flagging the student as special custody, the special custody icon will appear in the student’s profile area, and hovering over the icon will show the contents of the note as shown here:

 

 

This note also prominently appears on the Summary page when a student's record is first accessed.

Legal guardian/custodian contacts

You can flag a student’s contact as a legal guardian or custodian on the Family & contacts page, Custody card.  These contacts will appear in separate contacts cards when the Custody submenu option is selected in the student's record.

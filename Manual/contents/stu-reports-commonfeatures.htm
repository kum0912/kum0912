Student reports - Common features

 

Overview

Student reports collect information for a group of students and present that information in a report that you can print or save. To access student reports, choose Student reports from the menu.

  

 Here's how the Student reports navigation works:

 

  • click Student reports menu option and you will be taken directly to a cards view of all available reports.
  • click the drop down arrow beside Student reports menu option and you will see a list of reports.

 

The available student reports will be organized into groups of similar types of reports, and cards will appear for each available report.   

  

Select the card to access the report, or if the report includes buttons for THIS YEAR and NEXT YEAR, select one of the buttons corresponding to whether you want a report on information for this year or the next year. Students have different grades, homerooms, advisor groups, classes, and timetables for this year and next year. By selecting the appropriate button, the report will use the information the student has assigned for the selected year. The student’s Maplewood status will be used to determine whether the student is included in a report for this year or next year.

Common features for selecting variables.

Once you've selected a report there will be some additional report settings available as you navigate through the different options on the tab strip shown below. The FILTER tab is defaulted so that you may first select who you want printed. We will use an Alphabet example report.

 

The default setting for the other tab strip options will be based on the settings you used the last time you printed the report. Your settings are unique to you, so they may be different than the settings used by another person.

 

Here’s what you specify on each tab:

 

Filter:  Select your student(s) to include; specify first and last.
Next select the registered type, both this year and next year. This year only (not returning), Next year only or Withdrawn (no longer enrolled this year). Specify any additional options such as Each surname initial on a separate page, and Double spaced.

 

 

 

Name formats: Specify to print the student’s name using their usual name or full given and middle names. You can also include the student number on the report.

 

 

 

Student information: Here you can specify add additional student data to the report, such as Personal, Enrolment, Contact phone, Defined character and Locker info.

 

 

Bus: include the student’s bus Inbound and Outbound information on the report, if appropriate.

Inbound options are First route, Second route, and Comments. Outbound options are First route, Second route, Late bus and comments.

 

Font: customize fonts used in the report for the header/footer, title, student name, and student information. For each area, you may select a unique font name, font style (e.g. regular, bold, italic, etc.), and font size. Fonts installed on the server are available for selection. This is also where the date in the footer may be turned off or set to the operating system or school date.

 

Some reports, such as class and homeroom lists, provide the ability to print an attendance entry grid for a specific month in the school year (with X’s on those dates where the student is not enrolled) or a blank grid (for manual mark entry or any other purpose the teacher may want). These options are available on the Filter tab if applicable, and if selected, the Student information tab is disabled.

 

Some reports, such as student timetables, have unique setup options rather than the tab strip described above. Regardless of whether the report settings are presented on the initial page or in a tab strip, the report settings are self-explanatory and vary depending upon the report selected. We encourage you to try different settings and then see the resulting reports. Some examples are included below to illustrate the flexibility of the reports.

Overview

 

 

The incidents feature in provides a way to record detailed information about school incidents where disciplinary action is usually involved.  A single incident may involve one or many students. Common incident information such as the date, location, and time is entered, and then the students involved in the incident are recorded.  Each student involved has independent infraction and consequence information.  A student may have one or more infractions that are applied by selecting from a list of infractions (possible activities engaged in by a student that would breach the school/board/division’s set of rules such as fighting, bullying, possessing alcohol or illegal drugs, vandalism).  Similarly, the student may have one or more consequences (the result of the incident such as detention, suspension, expulsion).

 

Along with the infraction(s) and consequence(s), many additional fields are available to provide very detailed information on the incident. Many fields use standard field values (e.g. select from the table of values) so that the information may be analyzed and reported.

 

Incidents are available to all jurisdictions, all students (grades J-12), all Maplewood statuses (past, present, future students).  Ontario schools have one additional ‘card’ for tracking Suspension/expulsion program information.

 

You can access Incident info in 2 ways:

 

  • The Incidents menu
  • Student records menu.  

 

Before a school can start recording information about student incidents, a number of tables must first be setup. This document assumes these tables are already set up.

Ontario > Ontario reporting > Ontario Student Record (OSR) - French card

 

French card French Card (Junior Kindergarten to Grade 8) A Ministry of Education requirement, the French Card is an individual record of accumulated instruction in French as a second language. This card must be maintained for each student in an elementary school and stored within the Ontario Student Record (OSR). The information on the student's accumulated hours of instruction must be recorded on a card (identical to the one below). Any entry must be made on the record

 

  • at the end of each school year, semester or summer school course
  • when a student transfer to another school, including private, federal or First Nations School
  • when the student retires form the school

edsembli automatically populates and stores the entries for this card, allowing the schools to simply `Print’ the card for the OSR, rather than maintaining a manual record.

 

The edsembli administrative user can view or edit this information by accessing the student’s record.

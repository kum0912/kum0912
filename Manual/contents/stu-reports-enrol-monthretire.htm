Student reports > Enrolment > Monthly retirements

 

Monthly retirements  

This report provides a list of students who have left the school during any given month of the current school year. The student’s retirement/exist date from the school is used to determine when a student has retired from a school.

 

To create a Monthly retirements report, choose Student reports > Enrolment and select RUN on the Monthly retirements card.

 

 

The Student reports - Monthly retirements screen will appear.

 

 

Other - You'll see these fields:

 

Month: The month to be used in the report. Since this report is based on information at the end of the month, it will consider each student’s entry and exit dates to determine whether the student left the school during the selected month. Include retirements after last day of school: (Only available when printing the last month in the school’s calendar)  Y/N field to indicate whether to include students who have left your school after the last day of school. For example, if school is done in June, students who are given a retirement/exit date of July 1 may be included if this field is set to Yes.

 

Include: (Does not appear for elementary schools)  All students will be included by default. Secondary schools will have the option to generate separate reports for elementary or secondary students by changing this field to Elementary students or Secondary students. The student’s entry date to secondary school will be used to determine whether the student is considered elementary or secondary on the specified date. If this report is run centrally, the report may include either all students, elementary students, or secondary students.

 

Include retirements after last day of school: Check to include or leave unchecked to exclude.

 

The resulting report will include student information from the student’s record. The student’s age is the age of the student on retirement/exit date.

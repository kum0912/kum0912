 Overview

Staff reports collect information for a group of staff and present that information in a report that may be printed or saved. To access staff reports, select the Menu icon in the left corner, then select Staff tools > Staff reports from the menu.

  

 

 

The available staff reports will be organized into groups of similar types of reports.  For each group of reports, a card will appear for each available report.

 

  

Select the card to access the report, or if the report includes buttons for THIS YEAR and NEXT YEAR, select one of the buttons corresponding to whether you want a report on information for this year or the next year.

 Print Lists

The list section is where staff lists are available. 

The staff lists may consider the information data within staff records for this year or for next year. A page break may be optionally inserted between each staff member.  

There are a number of setup options presented on the initial staff print lists page. These options include selecting which staff is to be printed, whether the information be double spaced and/or indented for a 3 hole punch, and the addition of the date in the footer. 

 

There are also options regarding the name formats of the staff members on the print lists. As well as the type of staff information that will be included on the print lists.

Once the report settings are defined, use the Print floating action button shown below to view the resulting report.

 

 

Selecting the Printer icon will download a copy of the timetable for viewing, printing, or saving. At this point Opening or Saving before printing will act differently depending on your browser. If you are using Chrome the PDF may open in Adobe PDF reader or you may have the Chrome PDF plugin enabled. For more details on printing see Common features, Print.

 

 Print Timetables

The timetable section is where the teacher timetables are available.

Teacher timetable

The teacher’s timetable may consider the teachers’ data for this year or for next year. A page break may be optionally inserted between each teacher.  

There are a number of setup options presented on the initial teacher timetable page. These options include selecting who is to be printed, whether the timetable will print portrait or landscape, whether semesters print down or across, setting to define what information is to print for each block in the timetable, as well as a selection of what semester(s) and term(s) are to be printed.

The report settings are self-explanatory. We encourage you to try different settings and then see the resulting reports. Some examples are included below to illustrate the flexibility of the reports.

Overview

The Emergency menu option in a student’s record contains information that tends to be student-specific, in other words, information that is the same regardless of what school the student attends. Only the school that is considered the record owner can edit this information.

 

There is a Contacts menu option located in the Emergency submenu.

 

 

Use this Contacts option to see contact information for the student’s contacts that are flagged as emergency or school closure contacts as described below.

 

Contacts

The student’s contacts may be flagged as the student’s emergency contact and/or school closure contact. When the contact is flagged, they are also assigned a contact order for emergencies and/or a contact order for school closures. A contact card will appear for each contact with either of these flags. The card will indicate if the person is an emergency contact and/or a school closure contact, along with their respective contact orders. In addition to the contact’s name, contact type (e.g. Father), the contact’s phone and e-mail addresses are displayed as shown below.

Students without OENs

 

 

Use the Students without OENs option to determine whether the school has students who do not have OENs (Ontario Education Numbers) assigned.

 

The OEN is a student identification number that will be assigned by the Ministry of Education to elementary and secondary students across the province. The number, which is unique to every student, will be used as the key identifier on a student’s school records, and will follow the student through his or her elementary and secondary education. The OEN is nine digits long (eight digits, plus a check digit), randomly assigned, and tied to stable information about the student (name, gender, date of birth). OENs are different from the locally assigned student numbers (assigned by the school/board).  

 

Students must have OENs before elemental student data can be submitted to OnSIS, so this option should be run before commencing OnSIS reporting.   

 

Some students may legitimately have no OEN, for example,

 

  • New Ontario students, registered to attend publicly funded schools, should not have OENs requested/assigned until the student physically shows up present to class at the school.  

 

  • Non-inspected schools cannot request OENs. Students attending elementary private/native and non-inspected secondary private/native schools will not have OENs. OEN functionality is not applicable to these schools.

 

  • Continuing education and summer schools may have students that are really parents of a child attending school in the board.  These parents are taking a literacy and numeracy program to help support their child, and therefore they do not qualify for an OEN. They have a special link to their child and all Ministry reporting will use their child’s OEN.

 

Selecting the Students without OENs option will present any student without an OEN (other than past students who may have attended the school in previous years), along with the student’s grade this year and next year, and the student’s school year status.

 

Choose Ontario reporting, Ontario Education Numbers (OEN) from the main menu.

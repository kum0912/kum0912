Flags

 

Overview

Flags are available for all students (past, present and future students). The Flags menu option is located in the Enrolment submenu.

 

The board/division/district and the school can define Flags by choosing Setup > User-defined fields, then selecting GO on the Flags card.

 

 

The Flags menu option is used by the school to flag students for the school’s own purposes. Students may be flagged by coloured dots (referred to as flags) and by defined characters (4 letters or numbers that are assigned to the student to flag the student). Flags and defined characters appear in the student’s profile as shown below. Hovering over the flag will show the title assigned to the flag.

 

 

There is no limit to the number of flags that may be assigned to student, so you may have to use the navigation arrow to see more flags.

 

 

Each student has 4 defined characters, represented by 4 0’s if not entered for the student. The school defines what the 4 characters represent and they are “secret” to the school in that only school staff know what they mean. Each defined character may be a may be a letter A to Z, or a number 0 to 100. They are used as a quick means to identify groups of students. For example, a bilingual school may use defined character 1 to represent whether the student is English or French. Each student will have an E or F recorded in the 1st defined character.

 

Flags and defined characters are considered school-specific fields, allowing a school to uniquely flag the student to reflect their enrolment at that school. Flags assigned by one school will not impact a concurrently enrolled student’s information at another school.

 

Before you can assign flags, the board/division/district and/or the school has to define the flags. A flag is defined by setting the colour of the flag, determining if you want the flag to blink, and defining what the flag means. The title will appear as the tool-tip message when the user hovers.

 

Flag and defined character information is presented on a single card titled Flags, described below.  

 

Flags

The Flags card will show the flags and defined characters that are currently assigned to a student as shown here:

 

 

 

Use the Edit icon to access the Edit flags screen. The flags currently assigned to the student will have checked boxes. Add or remove flags by checking or unchecking the flag boxes.

 

 

 

Select the define character value from the drop down list; values range from A to Z and 0 to 100.

 

Defined characters should be used by the school so that the same meaning would apply to all applicable students (e.g. for everyone in the school defined character 1 represents whether the student is English or French). There are no tool-tips for defined characters.
